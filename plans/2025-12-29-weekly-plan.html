‚≠ê From Scratch Recipe This Week

Chana Masala (IP)

This classic Indian chickpea curry is the perfect "from scratch" recipe for this week. It uses fresh tomatoes, cilantro, garlic, and ginger from the farmers market, creating a deeply flavorful, aromatic curry using the Instant Pot. The layered spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala) and slow-building flavors make this an authentic, interesting recipe that goes beyond simple weeknight cooking. Making a double batch lets us freeze half for future backup meals, and the active cooking time is still manageable for a Monday when we have more energy. This introduces complex Indian spice combinations while using fresh market vegetables.
