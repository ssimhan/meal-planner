## From Scratch Recipe This Week

**Bisi Bele Bath** - This traditional South Indian one-pot rice and lentil dish is perfect for utilizing the farmers market vegetables, especially carrots, tomatoes, and onions. It's a comforting, flavorful meal that introduces the aromatic spice blend of sambar powder and the unique taste of tamarind. This recipe is a great way to start the week with a hearty, nutritious dinner that can be made in the Instant Pot or on the stovetop, providing a different cooking technique from the usual weeknight meals.
