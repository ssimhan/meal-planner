# Aloo Corn Tikki Roll

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 2tbspavocado oil
- 1tbspginger garlic paste
- 1-2 green chilies
- 3tbspKetchup
- 1/4tspTurmeric
- 1tspRed Chili powder
- 1tspCoriander powder
- 1tspCumin powder
- 1tspGaram masala
- 1/4cupwater
- 1/2cupcorn kernels
- 1largerusset potato
- Salt
- 1tspAamchur powder
- 2tbspFresh cilantro
- 2cupscurd
- 2tbspmint chutney
- 1smallred onion
- 1tbspFrankie masala
- 1tspvingar for topping
- Breadcrumbs (optional)

### Instructions
1. Boil and mash potatoes (peel and quarter, steam in IP for 8 min)
2. Fresh pan: saute oil, ginger, garlic, green chilies
3. Add ketchup (3 tbsp), turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala
4. Saute for 30 seconds
5. Add ¼ cup water and stir thoroughly
6. Cook for a minute
7. Add frozen corn kernels (½ cup), mashed potatoes (1 cup), salt, amchur powder
8. Mash and combine well
9. Add fresh coriander and let cool
10. Start rotimatic for chapathis
11. Shape into patty form and grill on both sides (high heat with minimal oil, add breadcrumbs if the patty won’t hold shape easily)
12. Make red sauce: curd, red chili powder, garlic juice, black salt
13. Make green sauce: curd (½ cup), mint chutney (2 tbsp)
14. Spread ghee on chapathi, add both sauces, chopped red onion and frankie masala
15. Add patty with a couple drops of vinegar and roll up
