# Apple Cinnamon Carrot Muffin

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 13/4 cup flour, wheat
- 11/2 tsp baking powder
- 1tspcinnamon
- 1/2tspbaking soda
- 1/2tspsalt
- 2 red apples, medium
- 1 carrot, large
- 3/4cupmilk
- 2 eggs, large
- 1/2cuphoney or agave
- 1/2cupavocado oil
- 1tspvanilla

### Instructions
1. Shred apples to get 1 1/2 cup for recipe, squeeze out extra moisture.Shred carrot to get 1/2 cup for recipe, squeeze out extra moisture.
2. Preheat over to 350 degrees and prepare muffin tins
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine dry ingredients. Mix in apples and carrots until well combined.
4. In a small bowl, combine wet ingredients. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix to incorporate ingredients.
5. Spoon into muffin tins. Bake for approximately 16 minutes or until atoothpick/knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
