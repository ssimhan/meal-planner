# Arisi Upma Mix

### Ingredients
- Basmati rice
- Toor dal
- Coconut oil
- Mustard seeds
- Urad dal
- Cumin
- Asoeftida
- Water
- Salt
- Fresh coconut, optional

### Instructions
1. Soak one cup of basmati rice and 3-4 teaspoons of toor dal for 10 min
2. Wash it and strain the water completely
3. Towel dry for one hour
4. Grind it in Preethi mixer to coarse consistency and store in glass jar in fridge
5. When ready to use...
6. Heat coconut oil or canola+ghee mixedAdd mustard, urad dhal, jeera, asoeftida and wait for crackle
7. For every half cup of mix, add 1.5 cups of water.
8. Add salt and fresh coconut to taste
9. Keep stirring until cooked, adding water if needed
