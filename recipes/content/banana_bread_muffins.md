# Banana Bread Muffins

### Ingredients
- 13/4 cup whole wheat flour
- 1tspbaking powder
- 1/2tspbaking soda
- 1tspcinnamon
- 3mediumripe bananas
- 1/4cuphoney or agave
- 1largeegg
- 1tspvanilla extract
- 1/2cupgreek yogurt
- 1/3cupmilk

### Instructions
1. Preheat over to 350 degrees and prepare muffin tins
2. In a bowl, mash bananas, then mix in cinnamon, maple syrup and vanilla.
3. Pour mixture into a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth andreturn to the bowl.
4. Add egg, yogurt and milk. Mix until combined.
5. In a separate bowl, mix together flour, baking soda and baking powder.
6. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until fully combined.
7. Pour batter into muffin tins and bake for ~15 minutes.
