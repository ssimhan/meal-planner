# Beans Poriyal

### Ingredients
- 20 Beans
- 1 medium Onion
- Sambar powder
- Turmeric
- ¼cup Grated coconut
- Salt
- To temper:
- Oil
- Mustard seeds
- Urad dal
- Curry leaves

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots

### Instructions
1. Chop beans to appropriate size.
2. Temper: oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves.
3. Add beans, sauté for 2 minutes in medium flame
4. Add sambar powder, turmeric and salt.
5. Sprinkle 3-4 tablespoon water and cook covered for 4-5 minutes.
6. Lastly add the grated coconut and stir once, remove from fire.
