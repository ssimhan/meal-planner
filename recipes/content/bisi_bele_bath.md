# Bisi Bele Bath

### Ingredients
- 1cuptoor dal
- 2cupsrice
- 1 medium onion
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 2 medium carrots
- 1cupbeans
- ½cup green peas
- 2smallpotatoes
- 2tbsptamarind pulp
- 4tbspBisi Bele Bath powder
- 1tspturmeric
- Salt
- water
- ghee
- 2tspmustard seeds
- 2tspcumin
- Curry leaves
- 4 dried red chilies
- 2tbspcashews

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Chop carrots

### Instructions
1. This recipe is for 4 adult lunch servings
2. Prep:Rinse 1 cup toor dal and 2 cups rice well.
3. Chop onions, tomatoes, carrots, beans, potatoes, and gather other ingredients.
4. Cook:Set the Instant Pot to ‘Sauté’ mode. Add 2 tbsp ghee, then mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, dried red chilies, and cashews. Sauté for 30 seconds.
5. Add onions and tomatoes, sauté until soft.
6. Then add all chopped vegetables. Mix in turmeric, salt, and 4 tbsp Bisi Bele Bath powder. Stir for 30 seconds.
7. Add rinsed toor dal and rice, followed by 8 cups water and 2 tbsp tamarind pulp. Mix well.
8. Cancel ‘Sauté’ mode, close the lid, and set Instant Pot to ‘Pressure Cook’ on HIGH for 10 minutes.
9. Allow pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
10. Open the lid, stir well, and adjust consistency by adding a little hot water if needed.
