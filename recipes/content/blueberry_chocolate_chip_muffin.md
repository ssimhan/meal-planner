# Blueberry Chocolate Chip Muffin

### Ingredients
- 1cupwheat flour
- 1/2cupbuckwheat flour
- 1/4cupother flour (wheat or all purpose)
- 1/2tspbaking powder
- 1/2tspbaking soda
- 1/2tspsalt
- 1/4cupsugar
- 1/3cupavocado oil
- 2 eggs
- 2/3cupmilk
- 2tspvinegar
- 2tspvanilla
- 1 medium sweet potato
- 1cupblueberries (frozen ok)
- 1/2cupchocolate chips

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and prep muffin tins
2. Mix milk with 2 tsp of vinegar and let it sit for a few minutes
3. Peel sweet potato and grate (only use 3/4 cup of shredded SP in recipe).
4. In a large mixing bowl, mix flours, baking power, baking soda, salt andsugar in a large mixing bowl.
5. In a separate mixing bowl, mix avocado oil, eggs, milk mixture, vanilla and sweet potato until smooth.
6. Pour liquid ingredients into dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed. Do not over mix.
7. Add blueberries and chocolate chips.
8. Pour into muffin tins.
9. Bake for 16 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
10. Cool before eating or freezing.
