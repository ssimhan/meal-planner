# Blueberry Date Oatmeal

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1cuprolled oats
- 2 Tbs frozen riced cauliflower
- 11⁄2 cup milk
- 6 dates
- 2 Tbs coconut oil
- 1⁄2 cup frozen blueberries

### Instructions
1. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix oats, cauliflower, milk and dates.
2. Microwave on high for approximately 6 minutes. Alternately, cook on stovetop, stirring frequently.
3. Stir in blueberries and coconut oil before serving
