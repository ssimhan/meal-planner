# Carrot Cake Bars

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1 banana
- 1 egg
- 1/4cuppeanut butter ⁣
- 1/4cupmaple syrup ⁣
- 1 medium carrot
- 1cupoat flour
- 1/2tspbaking powder ⁣
- 1/2tspcinnamon ⁣
- 1/4cupwhite chocolate chips (optional)⁣

### Instructions
1. Grate carrot and mash a ripe banana.
2. Preheat oven 180c⁣
3. In a prepared loaf tin, mix together the wet ingredients (banana, egg, peanut butter, maple syrup, carrot) and then the remaining ingredients. Mix well.⁣
4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean ⁣
