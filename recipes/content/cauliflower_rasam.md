# Cauliflower Rasam

### Ingredients
- Cauliflower florets (package)
- 2 medium tomatoes
- Cilantro
- 1cuptoor dal
- Shredded coconut
- Green chili (must have)
- Cumin seeds
- Ghee
- Mustard seeds
- Lemon juice

### Instructions
1. Boil florets with a little salt in hot water.
2. Drain when half done.
3. Immerse in water with salt, turmeric, 1 tomato and cilantro
4. While that boils, keep paruppu.
5. Grind coconut, green chili, half tomato, cumin, cilantro into paste
6. Add paruppu jelam and simmer for ten minutes.
7. Add tadka with ghee, cumin and mustard seeds.
8. Add lemon juice just before serving.
