# Cheesy Veggie Quesadilla

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1tbspoil
- 1tbspgarlic
- 1/4 th cup onion
- 1/2cupmixed bell pepper
- 1/2cupcorn
- 1/2cuppaneer
- 1tspsweet chilli sauce
- Salt to taste
- Black pepper for seasoning
- Oregano
- Red chilli flakes
- 1tbspOil
- Tortillas
- 8ozmozzarella cheese

### Instructions
1. Set a pan on a medium flame and add oil, garlic, onion, mixed bell pepper, corn, paneer, sweet chilli sauce, salt to taste, black pepper for seasoning, oregano and red chilli flakes
2. Mix well and cook for some time
3. In a fresh pan, add some oil, tortilla, veggie mixture and mozzarella.
4. Place an another tortilla over it and cook from both the sides as shown in the video
5. Cut it using pizza cutter and serve with mayo dip
