### Ingredients
- Idli or dosa batter
- 1 onion
- cilantro
- cumin
- salt
- garam masala

### Instructions
1. Saute onion until caramelized.Turn off heat and add chopped cilantroOnce cooled, add batter and mix.Add more batter because you'll want a lot more.
2. Add oil to kuzhiAdd battercover for 2 minutes on mediumflip over using spoonscover and cook again for 1 minute
3. Scoop out and enjoy
