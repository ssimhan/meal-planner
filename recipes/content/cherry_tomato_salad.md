# Cherry Tomato Salad

### Ingredients
- ¼cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons agave
- ½teaspoon minced garlic
- 4cupscherry tomatoes
- ¼cup chopped fresh oregano

## Prep Steps
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, agave, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Add the tomatoes and the oregano and toss to combine. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
