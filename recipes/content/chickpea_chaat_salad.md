# Chickpea Chaat Salad

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1canchickpeas
- 1 onion, red
- 1 bell pepper, green
- 1 medium cucumber
- 1 medium tomato
- 1 medium carrot
- Cilantro
- Mint
- Olive oil
- Sriracha
- Lemon juice
- Garlic
- Cumin powder
- Chaat masala
- Tamarind paste
- Salt
- Peanuts

### Instructions
1. Grate carrotDice onion, bell pepper, cucumber, tomatoChop and toast peanuts
2. Place chickpeas, onion, bell pepper, cucumber, tomato, carrot, red chilli, coriander and mint in a large bowl. Toss and mix well.
3. Prepare the dressing by mixing olive oil, sriracha, lemon juice, garlic, cumin powder, chaat masala, tamarind paste, salt.
4. When ready to serve, add the dressing to the salad in the bowl. Toss and mix well to coat everything evenly with the dressing.
5. Top with peanuts and extra cilantro
