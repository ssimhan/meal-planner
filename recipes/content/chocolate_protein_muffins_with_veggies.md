# Chocolate Protein Muffins (with Veggies!)

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- US Customary Metric
- 1cuppeanut butter
- 1cupbanana
- 1cupraw zucchini, or 2 nd banana
- 1cupspinach
- 3 eggs
- ¼cup honey
- Vanilla extract
- ¼cup cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons ground flaxseeds
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼teaspoon baking soda
- Chocolate chips
- ¼teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease the muffin tin.
2. Place all ingredients into a blender and blend until very smooth.
3. Fill muffin cups about ½ full using about ¼ cup batter in each. Top with chocolate chips, if using.
4. Bake 20 minutes for regular muffins and 12 for mini muffins.
5. Let cool for 5-10 minutes in the pan, run a knife around the edges, and carefully transfer to a wire rack to cool.
