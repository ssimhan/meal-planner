# Cilantro Lime Crema

### Ingredients
- 1cupwhole milk Greek yogurt or sour cream
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1smallgarlic clove
- ½teaspoon sea salt
- ¼teaspoon onion powder
- ½cup fresh cilantro

### Instructions
1. In a food processor, place the yogurt, mayo, lime juice, garlic, salt, and onion powder. Process until creamy.
2. Add the cilantro and pulse until the sauce is smooth but green flecks are still visible. Season to taste.
