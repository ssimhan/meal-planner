# Crispy Chipotle Sweet Potato Tacos with Lime Crema

### Ingredients
- Oil
- 2 medium sweet potatoes
- Red chili powder
- Paprika
- Cumin powder
- Garlic powder
- Crushed red pepper
- Oregano
- Salt
- 1 medium poblano pepper
- 1/2canblack beans
- 8 hard shell tacos
- 11/2 cups shredded Mexican cheese
- Cilantro
- 1 jalapeño
- 1/2cupGreek yogurt
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- zest of 1 lime
- sea salt

## Prep Steps
- Peel and cube sweet potatoes
- Chop peppers
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. Peel and cube the sweet potatoes.Seed and chop the poblano.
2. Preheat the oven to 425° F.
3. Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and toss with olive oil, chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, oregano, and salt.
4. Add the poblano and toss to combine.
5. Arrange in an even layer.
6. Bake 35 to 40 minutes, until the potatoes are tender and beginning to char on the edges.
7. Remove from the oven and stir in the black beans.
8. Meanwhile, make the crema.
9. Combine all ingredients in a glass jar and shake or stir until creamy.
10. Line the taco shells up on a sheet pan or in a 9×13 inch baking dish.
11. Transfer to the oven and bake 5 minutes. Evenly divide the sweet potatoes among each taco shell and top with cheese.
12. Bake 10 minutes, until the cheese has melted.
13. Mix the cilantro and jalapeño in a small bowl. Serve the tacos topped with crema, avocado, and the spicy cilantro. Enjoy!
