# Curried Cauliflower Quinoa Salad

### Ingredients
- 1/2cupGreek yogurt
- 1 medium cucumber
- Olive oil
- 1/3cuptahini
- Lemon juice
- Garlic
- Salt
- 1smallhead cauliflower
- Cilantro
- 2cupquinoa
- 2cupred cabbage
- 4cuparugula

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
2. Cook the quinoa. Dice all the cucumbers.
3. In a large bowl toss cauliflower with olive oil, desired spices (cumin, coriander, etc) and salt. Spread onto a baking sheet in a single layer and bake on center rack for 40 minutes.
4. While the cauliflower is cooking prepare the salad dressing by combining yogurt, tahini, cucumber, oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt in a blender and blending until smooth.
5. In a large bowl toss together cooked quinoa, red cabbage and arugula and divide between 4 bowls.
6. Top each bowl with fresh cilantro, cucumbers, golden raisins and roasted cauliflower.
7. Drizzle with yogurt tahini dressing and enjoy!
