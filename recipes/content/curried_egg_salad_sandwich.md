# Curried Egg Salad Sandwich

### Ingredients
- 1cupmayo
- 3clovesgarlic, minced
- 1/2tspred chili powder
- 1.5 tsp curry powder
- 1/2tspgaram masala
- 1/4cupcilantro, finely chopped
- 6 boiled eggs, chopped
- Bread, croissant or crackers

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, mix together all of the ingredients except the eggs.
2. Add in the boiled chopped eggs, mix together well and serve!
3. Great on a croissant, between bread or on enjoy on crackers for a great snack.
