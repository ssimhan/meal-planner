# Date Cookies

### Ingredients
- 1cupmedjool dates
- 1/2cupbutter, melted
- 1largeegg
- 1tspvanilla
- 1/3cupbuckwheat flour
- 2/3cupwhite whole wheat flour
- 1cupoats
- 1/2tspbaking soda
- 1/2tspcinnamon
- 1/4cupgreek yogurt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and place parchment paper on cookie sheets.
2. Put dates and butter in a food processor. Process until smooth.
3. Add egg, Greek yogurt and vanilla to the food processor. Mix thoroughly.
4. Take a new bowl and mix buckwheat flour, white whole wheat flour, oats, baking soda, and cinnamon.
5. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and mix fully.
6. Use ice cream scooper to make small balls of dough. Place on greased cookie sheets and press down on the top.
7. Bake 12-15 min - until lightly brown.
