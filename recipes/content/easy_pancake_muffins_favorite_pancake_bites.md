# Easy Pancake Muffins (Favorite Pancake Bites)

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- US Customary Metric
- 1cupwhole-wheat flour (or all purpose)
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 3/4cupmilk
- 2 eggs (lightly beaten)
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted and cooled)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1cupmix-ins (such as blueberries, diced strawberries, diced bananas, chopped walnuts, or chocolate chips; if using chocolate chips, use 1/4-⅓ cup)

### Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F and grease a mini muffin pan with nonstick spray.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
3. Gently whisk in the eggs, vanilla, milk, and butter. Fold in the desired mix-in.
4. Divide batter among the prepared muffin pan.
5. Mini Muffins: Use 1 tablespoon batter in each muffin cup and bake for 12-14 minutes.
6. Standard Muffins: Use just under ¼ cup batter in each muffin and bake for 16-18 minutes. Check for doneness with a cake tester or toothpick. When inserted into the center, it should come out cleanly.
7. Serve with a dip such as applesauce, nut butter, yogurt, or maple syrup.
