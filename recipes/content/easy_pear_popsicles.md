### Instructions
1. Below is a look at the basic steps involved in making this popsicle recipe so you know what to expect from the process. Plan to start this 4-6 hours before you want to serve them so they have time to freeze.
2. AD
3. Pour the pears and juice into a bowl.
4. Roughly chop up with kitchen scissors or mash slightly with a potato masher.
5. Portion into popsicle molds.
6. Freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight. Release from the popsicle mold and serve.
7. How to Store
8. Transfer the frozen popsicles to a freezer bag, remove as much air as possible, and seal. Freeze for up to 6 months.
