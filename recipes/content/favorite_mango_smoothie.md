# Favorite Mango Smoothie

### Ingredients
- AD
- 1cupfrozen mango
- ½cup whole milk plain Greek yogurt (or regular yogurt)
- ½fresh large orange or 1 small orange (peeled; or ½ cup orange juice)
- Optional veggies: ½ cup raw cauliflower florets or sliced summer squash
- Optional seeds: 1 teaspoon hemp or ground flaxseed
- Optional sweetener: 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup

## Prep Steps
- Peel/chop squash
- Cut florets

### Instructions
1. AD
2. Gather the ingredients and any optional add-ins.
3. Blend, starting on low and working up to high.
4. Serve. You can also store it in the fridge and serve within 24 hours.
5. AD
6. Add all ingredients, including any add-ins, to a blender.
7. Blend until very smooth, stopping to scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed.
8. Serve or store in an airtight container or a reusable Squeasy Gear pouch, with the lid snapped, for up to 24 hours in the fridge.
