# Grilled Cheese

A classic comfort food sandwich.

## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread
- 2 slices cheddar cheese
- 1 tbsp butter

## Instructions

1. Butter one side of each bread slice
2. Place cheese between bread slices (butter side out)
3. Cook in skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides
4. Serve hot
