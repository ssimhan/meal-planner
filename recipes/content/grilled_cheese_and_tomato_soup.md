# Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- Wheat bread
- Cheese, sliced or shredded
- Butter
- Garlic powder (optional)
- 2 cartons Trader Joe's tomato soup
- 1/2cupmilk

### Instructions
1. Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each slice of bread.
2. (Optional) Sprinkle a pinch of garlic powde inside the sandwich for added flavor.
3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on the preheated pan.
4. Add the cheese slices on top, along with a slice of tomato if desired, then cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
5. Cook on medium heat for about 2-3 minutes on one side, pressing gently with a spatula, until golden brown.
6. Flip carefully and cook the other side for another 2-3 minutes until the bread is crispy and the cheese is fully melted.
7. Pour the Trader Joe's tomato soup into a small pot or microwave-safe bowl.
8. Heat on the stovetop for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, or microwave for 2-3 minutes.
9. For a creamier soup, stir in a splash of milk or cream while heating.
10. Cut the grilled cheese in half (diagonally for a classic touch!).
11. Pour the hot tomato soup into a bowl.
12. Pair your sandwich with the soup and enjoy dipping for extra flavor.
