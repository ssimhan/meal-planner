# Homemade Larabars

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1cupnuts any combination
- 1-½cups dates or a combination of dates and another variety of dried fruit
- ⅛teaspoon kosher salt

### Instructions
1. Place dates into a small heat-safe bowl. Pour hot water over the dates and allow to soak for 10-15 minutes to soften the dates.
2. Once the dates have soaked, drain the liquid off the dates, reserving the liquid to use if needed.
3. Fit a food processor with an s-blade. Place nuts in the food processor and pulse until the nuts break down into small pieces.
4. Add in the dates and dash of salt, and pulse the mixture together until the dates have broken down and a thick paste forms, scraping the sides down if needed. Add in 1 to 2 tablespoons of the soaking liquid if needed to help bring the mixture together.
5. To shape larabars press mixture into a 7×11 pan lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for 30 minutes before slicing int0 6 bars.
6. Alternatively, you can shape the mixture into 1-2 inch balls for a bite-size treat.
