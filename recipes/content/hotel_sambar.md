# Hotel Sambar

### Ingredients
- 1½cups toor dal, cooked
- 1cuptamarind extract
- Turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon jaggery
- 2 green chilies
- Curry leaves
- ½onion
- 5 green beans
- 1 medium carrot
- 1 medium tomato
- Salt
- Water
- Sambar powder
- Cilantro
- For Tempering:
- Oil
- Mustard seeds
- Dried red chili
- Curry leaves

## Prep Steps
- Chop carrots
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. **Prep:**Cook 1/2 cup of toor dal.
2. Chop carrot, potato, tomato, onion.
3. Soak tamarind: Take a small lemon-sized piece of tamarind and soak it in ¼ cup warm water for about 10 minutes. Then squeeze and strain to get the juice.
4. Cook:Take the chopped carrot, potato, and onion with 1 cup water.Boil until vegetables are soft and cooked through.
5. Add the chopped tomato to the cooked vegetables and cook for 2 minutes.
6. Add the mashed dal to the pot.
7. Stir in 1½ tablespoons sambar powder.
8. Add the tamarind extract and salt to taste.
9. Let everything simmer together for about 5 minutes.
10. Make the tempering (tadka):
11. In a small pan, heat 2 teaspoons oil.
12. Add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds and let them pop.
13. Add a pinch of asafoetida and 1 dried red chili, broken in half.
14. Let it sizzle for a few seconds, then turn off the heat.
15. Pour the tempering into the simmering sambar.
16. Mix well and let it simmer 2 more minutes.
17. Add cilantro for garnish.
