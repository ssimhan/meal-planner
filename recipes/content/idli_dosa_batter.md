# Idli Dosa Batter

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1CupUrad Dal
- 3CupsIdli Rice
- 1/4 tablespoon Salt
- 1 tablespoon fenugreek
- 1/2tspbaking soda or baking powder

### Instructions
1. Rinse the Urad Dal and methi in water for 2-3 times.
2. Rinse the rice separately 2-3 times.
3. Soak the Urad dal and methi in one bowl, and Rice in another bowl for minimum 4 hours but ideally overnight.
4. Preheat oven to 200F.
5. Grind the Urad dal with minimum water to fine and fluffy consistency.
6. Grind the rice with minimum water.
7. Add 1/4 tablespoon of salt and mix everything with hand for at least 2-3 minutes.
8. Add 1/4 to 1/2 tsp. of baking soda (or baking powder) while you mix the batter if it's a very cold day.
9. Turn off oven and place batter for 10-24 hours.
