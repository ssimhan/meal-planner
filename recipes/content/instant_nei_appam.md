# Instant Nei Appam

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1cupRice flour
- ¼cup ghee
- 1cupjaggery, powder
- 1 banana, ripe
- 1 tablespoon wheat flour
- ⅛teaspoon Salt

### Instructions
1. In a blender, place banana, jaggery or brown sugar, cardamom, salt and grind smoothly.
2. Add rice flour and wheat flour to it along and blend again.
3. No need to add water. If needed only add later maximum 1 or 2 tbsp. Batter should be like pancake batter or idli dosa batter consistency.
4. Transfer to the bowl. Heat appe pan/ paniyaram pan and generously drizzle with ghee.
5. Pour the batter in the shallows (kuzhi) and cook covered in medium flame.
6. Flip and cook again to cook until golden on both sides. Add ghee more on top if desired.
