# IP Quinoa Chili

### Ingredients
- 1 medium onion
- 1 bell pepper, green
- 3clovesgarlic
- 2/3cupquinoa
- 14.5 ounce can Fire-roasted diced Tomatoes
- 1canblack beans
- 1cupfrozen corn
- Red chili powder
- Paprika
- Cumin powder
- Salt
- 2cupbroth
- Cilantro
- Lemon wedges
- Sour cream

### Instructions
1. Prep:Dice 1 cup yellow onion.Dice 1 cup green bell pepper.Finely dice ½ a jalapeño.Mince 3 cloves garlic.Wash ⅓ cup quinoa.Rinse and drain 1 can black beans (about 1.25 cups).
2. Cook
3. Add all the ingredients to the Instant Pot in this order:Diced onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, and garlic.
4. Washed quinoa, 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), 1 cup frozen corn, and the rinsed black beans.
5. Add ¾ tablespoon chili powder, ½ tablespoon paprika, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, and 1 teaspoon salt (adjust later to taste).
6. Pour in 2 cups vegetable broth.
7. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the vent to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.
8. Release Pressure: Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally (this might take 10-15 minutes). Open the lid and taste the chili. Adjust the salt if necessary.
9. Serve: Garnish with chopped cilantro. Add any optional toppings you like:
10. Shredded cheddarAvocado, lemon wedges, or diced red onionSour cream or greek yogurtServe with tortilla chips
11. Refrigerate: Store in an airtight container for up to 4 days.Freeze: Portion into small containers and freeze. Reheat in the microwave or on the stove when ready to eat.
