# Jeera Rice

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- using cooked basmati rice (way 1):
- ½cup basmati rice
- 1tspoil
- ½tsp salt
- 1tspghee / clarified butter
- 1tspcumin / jeera
- 2tbspcoriander (finely chopped)
- water (to soak & boil)
- using pressure cooker (way 2):
- 1tspghee / clarified butter
- 1tspcumin / jeera
- ½cup basmati rice
- 1cupwater
- ¼tsp salt
- 2tbspcoriander (finely chopped)

### Instructions
1. using cooked basmati rice:
2. now in a large heat 1 tsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp cumin seeds.
3. now add in cooked basmati rice, ¼ tsp salt and mix gently.
4. finally, add 2 tbsp coriander leaves and enjoy jeera rice with dal.
5. using pressure cooker:
6. firstly, in a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp cumin seeds.
7. now add ½ cup basmati rice (soaked 20 minutes) and saute for a minute.
8. add 1 cup of water, ¼ tsp salt and mix well.
9. cover and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
10. finally, add 2 tbsp coriander leaves and enjoy jeera rice with dal.
