# Kondakadalai Kala Channa Masala Sundal

### Ingredients
- 1cupkala chana
- Salt
- Asafoetida
- ¼cup Grated coconut
- To roast and powder
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal
- 1 teaspoon Coriander seeds
- ½teaspoon Pepper
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
- 2 Red chilli
- Asafoetida
- 1 teaspoon Oil
- To temper:
- 2 teaspoon Oil - 2 tsp
- ½teaspoon Mustard
- 2 teaspoon Urad dal - 2 tsp
- 8 Curry leaves

### Instructions
1. To start with, wash and soak chana overnight.
2. Next day, drain water and add just enough water to immerse the chana completely.
3. Then, add salt and asafoetida and pressure cook for 5 whistles.
4. Roast and grind:Chana dal, coriander seeds, pepper, red chili, little oil
5. Add cumin and asafoetida lastly as it will burn.
6. Roast until chana dal turns golden in colour and aroma wafts. Cool down and powder.
7. Temper:Oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves
8. Drain the cooked chana and add to the temper pot. Add the powdered masala and grated coconut.
9. Mix well for 2 minutes in medium flame.
10. Quick option: You can make this same without the powder too. Just temper with 2 red chillies.
