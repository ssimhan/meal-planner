# Loaded Greek Chickpea Pasta Salad

### Ingredients
- 8 ounce box of pasta
- 1(15 ounce) can chickpeas
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 yellow bell pepper
- 11/2 cups cherry tomatoes
- 1 cucumber, large diced
- 1cupfeta, crumbled
- 3/4cupkalamata olives
- Parsley
- 1/3cupolive oil
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1clovegarlic
- 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon maple syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon oregano
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Prep Steps
- Chop peppers
- Slice cucumber
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. Prep
2. Dice bell peppers and cucumber.
3. Halve cherry tomatoes.
4. Chop kalamata olives
5. CookPrepare all vegetables and place them in a large bowl with the feta, chickpeas and parsley.
6. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and add to the bowl.
7. Add all dressing ingredients to a small bowl and whisk until combined.  This includes olive oil, wine vinegar, garlic, dijon mustard, maple syrup, oregano, salt and pepper.
8. Pour dressing over pasta salad and mix all ingredients together until itâ€™s combined.
9. Pasta salad can be served immediately or refrigerated up to 3 days.
10. If turning into Mason jar salad, layer asdressing, pasta, veggies, chickpeas, feta.
