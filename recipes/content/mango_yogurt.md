# Mango Yogurt

### Ingredients
- 1cupfresh or frozen mango
- 1/2cupMango Puree
- 1cupgreek yogurt
- 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
- 1/2 teaspoon fresh lime zest

### Instructions
1. If making with fresh mango: Add fruit to a blender and blend smooth.
2. If making with frozen mango: Thaw at room temperature or warm for about 1 minute in the microwave. Add them to the blender and blend smooth.
3. If making with mango puree, add directly.
4. Stir together with the yogurt to make a uniform mixture.
5. Add any optional ingredients (lime juice, lime zest)
6. Stir and serve with granola.
