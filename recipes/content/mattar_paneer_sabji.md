# Mattar Paneer Sabji

### Ingredients
- 2tbspoil
- 2 onions
- 4clovesgarlic
- 4 medium tomatoes
- 2tbspcashew
- Oil
- Ghee
- 1tspcumin
- 2 green chili
- Turmeric
- Red chili powder
- Coriander powder
- Cumin powder
- Garam masala
- 3cupwater
- Salt
- 1½cup peas
- 200 grams paneer
- 2tbspmilk
- 1tspkasuri methi
- Cilantro

### Instructions
1. **Prep:**Roughly chop onion and tomatoes.Cube paneerCook the frozen peas in the microwave.
2. CookHeat oil, ginger, garlic. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add 4 tomatoes, 2 tbsp cashew, and saute until the tomato turn soft and mushy.
4. Cool completely, and transfer to a mixer jar.
5. Grind to smooth paste without adding water.
6. In the pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. add 1 tsp ghee, 1 tsp cumin, and 2 chilli. Saute until the spices turn aromatic.
7. Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, and ½ tsp garam masala.
8. Saute until the spices turn aromatic.
9. Add prepared gravy and cook until the oil separates from the gravy base.
10. Add 3 cup water and 1 tsp salt.
11. Add 1½ cups peas and paneer cubes.
12. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
13. Add 2 tbsp cream, 1 tsp kasuri methi, and 2 tbsp coriander.
14. Serve with roti or rice.
