# Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 11⁄2 cups cooked quinoa
- 1canwhite beans
- 1largecucumber
- 1largetomato
- 1/2canblack olives
- 1⁄4 cup feta cheese (optional)
- 1 scallion, diced
- 1⁄4 cup olive oil
- 2 Tbs. lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, mix quinoa, beans, cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, feta and scallion.
2. In a small bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper together.
3. Pour dressing over salad and mix gently.
