# Mexican Papdi Chaat

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 15ozcan black beans
- 15ozcan pinto beans
- 1 bell pepper, red
- 1 bell pepper, green
- Taco seasoning
- 1/2 medium onion
- 1cupfrozen corn
- 1/2cupscallions
- Cumin powder
- Red chili powder
- 2CupsTaco Sauce
- 1/2cupsalsa
- Cilantro
- Shredded Mexican cheese
- Cilantro chutney
- Tamarind chutney
- Chips or sev

### Instructions
1. PrepDice bell pepper, onion, scallionsCook corn
2. Mix black beans, pinto beans, bell pepper, taco seasoning, corn, scallions, cumin powder, red chili powder, taco sauce, salsa, cilantro.
3. If serving immediately, add onion and cheese.
4. Serve with chutney and chips
