# Mexican Risotto (IP)

### Ingredients
- 2cupsarborio rice
- Garlic
- 2 jalapenos
- Cilantro
- 6cupsvegetable broth
- 1/2 red onion
- Taco seasoning
- 1 packet chipotle corn from Trader Joe's
- 1/2cupmonterey jack cheese
- Oil
- Butter

## Prep Steps
- Mince garlic
- Wash and chop herbs
- Chop onions/shallots

### Instructions
1. PrepRoughly chop the cilantro.Grate the cheese.Leave frozen corn out at room temp to defrost a bit.
2. Make
3. Saute the onions in the oil and butter.
4. After onions have started to caramelize, add the rice, garlic, and jalape√±o. Stir occasionally until the rice is translucent (1 - 2 min).
5. Add broth, taco seasoning, spices and set the instant pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
6. Heat up the entire packet of chipotle corn with sauce pellets in pan on stove.
7. Manually release the pressure when done. Add the corn, cheese and half the cilantro. Stir to combine.
8. Top with cilantro to eat.
