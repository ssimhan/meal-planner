# Mexican Street Corn Pasta Salad

### Ingredients
- 2cupssmall pasta
- Olive oil
- 3cupscorn
- 2clovesgarlic
- 1/2 jalapeño, ribs removed
- 1bunchcilantro
- 1bunchscallions
- 1/4cupcotija cheese, crumbled
- 1/2cupsour cream
- 1/2cupmayo
- 2 chipotle peppers in adobo
- 1/3cupfresh lime juice
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Prep Steps
- Chop peppers

### Instructions
1. Prep:
2. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
3. Mince garlic, jalapeño, and chop cilantro and scallions (reserve some for garnish).
4. Crumble cotija cheese (reserve some for garnish).
5. Cook:
6. Heat olive oil in a skillet, add corn in a single layer, season with salt and pepper, and cook undisturbed for 5 minutes until charred.
7. Add garlic, cook for 1–2 more minutes, and cool.
8. Blend sour cream, mayonnaise, chipotle peppers, lime juice, and zest to make the dressing. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Assemble:
10. Combine pasta, charred corn, jalapeño, cilantro, scallions, and cotija cheese in a large bowl.
11. Toss with the dressing and adjust seasoning if needed.
12. Garnish with reserved cilantro, scallions, and cotija cheese. Serve or refrigerate.
