# Moong Dal Chilla

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1cupMoong Dal
- 2 Green Chili
- 1 teaspoon Ginger
- 1 medium onion
- 1 medium carrot
- Paneer, optional
- Cilantro
- Salt
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- ½teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder
- Water
- Oil

### Instructions
1. Add moong dal to a medium bowl. Rinse the moong dal and soak for 3+ hours (ideally overnight).
2. If desired, chop onion, grate carrot and paneer.
3. Drain the water. Transfer dal to a blender. Add about ⅔ cup water. Blend to make a smooth batter.
4. Transfer the batter to a bowl. Add green chili, ginger, onion, carrot, cilantro, salt, turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well, and add 1-2 tablespoons more water if needed. Make sure the batter is not too thick or too runny, it should have a pouring consistency.
5. Make chilla like you would make dosa, adding grated paneer when semi-solid.
6. Serve right way with chutney or tomato ketchup, and a cup of chai!
