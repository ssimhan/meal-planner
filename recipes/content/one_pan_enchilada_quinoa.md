# One Pan Enchilada Quinoa

### Ingredients
- 20ozenchilada sauce
- 1cupquinoa
- 1smallonion
- 1 bell pepper, red
- 1 medium jalapeno
- Cumin seeds
- Taco seasoning
- 1/4cupchopped scallions
- 2clovesgarlic
- Oil
- Sour cream
- 1/4cupshredded mexican cheese

### Instructions
1. Chop onion, peppers.
2. 1 cup quinoa is 3 kid servings. Make double if the adults are also eating
3. Heat a large skillet or pot over medium high heat and add oil.
4. Add cumin to the pan and let splutter. Add jalape√±os and cook for about 1 minute.
5. Add onions, peppers, garlic and taco seasoning to the pan and saute until onions and peppers are slightly softened after 8 minutes
6. Add quinoa, enchilada sauce and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover lid and let cook for about 15 minutes. Check in between and stir if necessary (add splash of water if needed).
7. Once the quinoa has cooked, add in the scallions, cheese, sour cream and serve immediately while hot!
