# Oven-Baked Crispy Black Bean Tacos

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- Olive oil
- 1/2 medium onion
- 3clovesgarlic
- 2 tbsp. Tomato Paste
- 2cansblack beans
- Taco Seasoning
- Lime juice
- Corn tortillas
- 1/2cupGreek Yogurt
- Cilantro
- Lime juice
- Olive oil
- Paprika
- Salt
- Pepper

### Instructions
1. **For the Black Bean Tacos:**Preheat oven to 450F. Line a baking sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. In a skillet over medium high heat, add the olive oil to heat up for 1 minute.
3. Add the minced garlic, onion, and jalape√±o peppers. Sauce for 1 minute, until fragrant.
4. Add the drained black beans and tomato paste. Stir to combine.
5. Add 1 cup water, stir to combine, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer.
6. Using a fork, lightly smash the black beans. Add more water as necessary to keep the black beans moist but not liquid-y.
7. Add the taco seasoning, lime juice, and salt and black pepper to taste.
8. Evenly distribute and spread over the tortillas. Place on the prepared baking pan.
9. Brush each tortilla with olive oil.
10. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until lightly golden brown, flip the tortillas over and bake another 8-10 minutes. Enjoy warm!
11. **For the Cilantro Yogurt Sauce:**Blend all ingredients together in a food processor or high speed blender until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the fridge up to 4 days.
