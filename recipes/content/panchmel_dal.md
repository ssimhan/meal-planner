# Panchmel Dal

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1.25 cups mixed dal
- 2 tablespoon ghee
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 3 red chilies
- 2 green chili
- ¼teaspoon Asafoetida
- 1 medium onion
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 4cupsWater
- Lime juice
- Cilantro
- ½teaspoon turmeric
- 2 teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder
- ½teaspoon Garam Masala
- 2 teaspoon Coriander powder
- Salt

### Instructions
1. Wash the lentils with water and keep aside. Finely dice onion and tomato. Set water in kettle for boiling.
2. Start the instant pot in sauté mode and heat the ghee/oil in it.
3. Add cumin, red chilies, green chili and asafoetida. Allow them to cook for 30 seconds while stirring constantly.
4. Then add diced onion and grated ginger. Sauté until onions are translucent for about 3 minutes.
5. Now add tomatoes and sauté for 3 minutes until tomatoes are soft and mushy. Add all the spices and give it a stir.
6. Add washed lentils and 4 cups water. Press CANCEL and close the instant pot lid with vent in sealing position.
7. Press MANUAL or PRESSURE COOK mode for 8 minutes and let the pressure release naturally (NPR)
8. Open the lid, add garam masala and lime juice.
9. Garnish with cilantro and serve. Enjoy!
10. Stovetop Method
11. Follow the same steps as above to cook the dal in a stovetop pressure cooker. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles until the dal is cooked and soft. Then let the pressure release naturally. Stir in garam masala and lime juice. Garnish with cilantro and serve!
