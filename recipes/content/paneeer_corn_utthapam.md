# Paneeer & Corn Utthapam

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 1/2cupRava
- 1/4cupcurd
- 1/4cupwater
- Salt to taste
- Red chili powder or chili flakes
- 1/2tspcumin powder
- 1/2tsppizza seasonings
- Handful paneer cubes diced small
- 1/4cupboiled corn
- 1/2 medium onion chopped
- 1tbspcoriander leaves

### Instructions
1. Mix all and keep aside for 10 minutes, add a spoon or two water if needed
2. Cut 2 large capsicum into little thick slices
3. Take a non stick or a cast iron pan, spread oil once heats, layer the rings fill with uttapam batter properly
4. Let cook in low medium flame until browns, flip and cook the other side as well. It should brown properly
5. Enjoy with ketchup or chutney
