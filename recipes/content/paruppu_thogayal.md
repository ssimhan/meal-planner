### Instructions
1. Fry toor dal with red chillies, with little oil till golden brown in medium flame or low flame.
2. Cool down and grind it to a coarse paste with added salt, peeled garlic and required.
3. You can add 2 tablespoon of coconut while grinding, that will give you an enhanced taste!
4. A pinch of tamarind is also a good idea.
5. To eat with rice, mix this paste with rice and little sesame oil or you can have as an accompaniment for vatral kuzhambu rice or curd rice!
