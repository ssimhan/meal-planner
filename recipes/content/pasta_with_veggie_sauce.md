# Pasta with Veggie Sauce

### Ingredients
- 1 beetroot
- 1 medium potato
- 1 medium onion
- Cashews, soaked
- Salt
- Cumin powder
- Pasta, penne

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots

### Instructions
1. Soak handful or two of cashews in small stainless steel bowl
2. Peel and quarter all veggies (can also use minimal carrot)
3. Boil water for pasta (1L+)
4. Place 2 cups water in IP pot with trivet on top
5. Balance the veggies on top of trivet and nut bowl on top of that
6. Set IP on manual for 5 minutes (double check online)
7. Cook pasta while IP is working
8. Quick release IP once done
9. Blend all veggies and nuts
10. Add salt, cumin powder, pepper and other spices as needed to make it tasty
11. Mix sauce with pasta and serve
