# Peanut Chutney

### Ingredients
- Â½cup Peanuts
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal
- 1 tablespoon Urad dal
- 5 green chilies
- Garlic
- Salt
- Oil
- Mustard seeds
- Urad dal
- 1 curry leaf

## Prep Steps
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. Dry roast peanuts in low flame to ensure even roasting.
2. Once its is crisp, transfer to a plate for cooling and roast channa and urad dal with a teaspoon of oil.
3. Lastly add green chillies and fry.
4. Cool everything.
5. Remove the skin of the peanuts completely.
6. Place in the mixer and grind with ginger (if desired), salt and water. Grind smoothly.
7. Adjust water consistency to make it runny.
8. Temper: oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaf.
9. Mix well.
