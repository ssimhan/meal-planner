# Pinto Bean Casserole

### Ingredients
- 1 red onion
- 2 bell peppers
- 5clovesgarlic
- 1tbspolive oil
- 2canspinto beans
- 15ozcan petite diced tomatoes
- 1cupfrozen corn
- 4cupsfresh baby spinach, chopped
- 1tbspchili powder
- 1tspground cumin
- 1tspdried oregano
- 1tsptaco seasoning
- Â½cup cooked quinoa
- 1 lime
- 1 medium avocado
- Cilantro
- Yogurt or sour cream, optional

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Chop peppers
- Wash and chop greens
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. Add onion, bell peppers, garlic and olive oil to a large pan over medium heat. Saute until onions are translucent, about 5-10 minutes.
2. Add pinto beans, diced tomatoes, corn, baby spinach and all spices (chili powder through black pepper). Saute another 10-15 minutes until everything is cooked through. Stir in quinoa.
3. Squeeze lime juice on top and stir to combine. Serve warm, topped with avocado, cilantro and greek yogurt.
