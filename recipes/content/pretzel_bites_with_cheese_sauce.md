# Pretzel Bites with Cheese Sauce

### Ingredients
- 1TbspButter
- 1TbspFlour
- 1cupWhole Milk
- 1cupCheddar Cheese shredded
- 1/2tspSalt
- 3/4cuplukewarm water (100°F – 110°F)
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- 1tspinstant or active dry yeast
- 2cupsflour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Olive oil
- 1/4cupbaking soda

### Instructions
1. **Pretzel Bite- Instructions**
2. Preheat the oven to 450 degree F. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper
3. Warm water in a pot until lukewarm. Add it to  yeast and whisk.
4. Sprinkle  sugar in it and whisk again. Set aside to let the yeast “bloom.”
5. Measure flour,  salt, and add to a mixing bowl with yeast mix.
6. If dough is too dry, add more water 1 tablespoon at a time and if the dough is too wet, add more flour 1 tablespoon at a time. Place dough in a lightly oiled large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise to minimum 10 min
7. Pour 6 cups of water into the pot and add baking soda. Bring to a boil over high heat.
8. Divide the dough into 3 pieces. Roll each piece into a 20 to 24-inch long rope. Cut into bite-size pieces.
9. Using a slotted spoon, gently place the bite size pieces  into the boiling water for 45 seconds. Remove the pretzels with a slotted spoon and drain well. Place onto the parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
10. Melt butter in the oven. Generously brush with melted butter on all sides. Sprinkle with coarse salt.
11. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the pretzels are golden brown. While the preServe with Sauce for dipping!
12. **Cheese sauce**In a small saucepan set over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour and cook for 30 seconds, whisking constantly.
13. Slowly whisk in the milk until no lumps of flour remain.
14. Stirring constantly, bring the milk to a simmer over medium-low heat until it thickens, about 3 to 5 minutes.
15. Remove the pan from the stove and stir in the shredded cheese until all of the cheese has melted.
16. Add a pinch of salt, if needed. Serve warm.
