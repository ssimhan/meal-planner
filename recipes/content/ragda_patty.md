# Ragda Patty

### Ingredients
- 1cupvatana
- 1 medium potato
- 3cupwater
- oil
- 1 medium onion
- 1tspginger garlic paste
- 1 chilli, finely chopped
- 2 medium tomatoes
- turmeric
- red chilli powder
- coriander powder
- cumin powder
- garam masala
- salt
- cilantro

### Instructions
1. pressure cook vatana, potato, turmeric, salt and water for 5 whistles.
2. Chop one onion and two tomatoes.
3. now in a large kadai heat 3 tsp oil and saute 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and 1 chilli.
4. also saute 1 tomato until it turns soft and mushy.
5. furthermore add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ¼ tsp garam masala and ¼ tsp salt.
6. saute on low flame until the oil is released from sides.
7. now add boiled peas and mix well.
8. boil for 5 minutes or until the spices is absorbed.
9. mash the peas and potato slightly, to thicken the consistency.
10. Top with cilantro
