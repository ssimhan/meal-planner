# Rasam Recipe

### Ingredients
- 2 medium tomatoes
- ½cup toor dal
- Water
- Tamarind concentrate
- 1–2 green chilies
- 1tspjaggery
- 1tsprasam powder
- Salt
- Oil
- Mustard seeds
- Cumin seeds
- 1 dried red chili
- Hing
- Curry leaves
- Cilantro

### Instructions
1. Rinse ½ cup toor dal until the water runs clear.
2. Chop 2 medium tomatoes.
3. In a pressure cooker, add the rinsed dal, chopped tomatoes, 1½ cups water, 1 tsp tamarind concentrate, slit green chilies, 1 tsp jaggery, 1 tsp rasam powder, and salt.
4. Close the lid and cook on medium heat for 3 to 4 whistles.
5. Let the pressure release naturally. Open the lid and gently mash the dal and tomatoes.
6. In a small pan, heat 1 tsp ghee. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
7. Add cumin seeds, dried red chili, a pinch of hing, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
8. Pour the tempering into the rasam. Stir to combine.
9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
