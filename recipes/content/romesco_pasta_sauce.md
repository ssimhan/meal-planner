# Romesco Pasta Sauce

### Ingredients
- 3 red peppers
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 8clovesgarlic
- 1/3cupalmonds
- Olive oil
- 7ozfirm tofu
- 3tbspnutritional yeast
- Broth for blending
- Fresh parsley
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper

## Prep Steps
- Chop peppers
- Press/cube tofu
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. Prep:Preheat your oven to 180°C (356°F).
2. Place the halved red peppers, tomatoes on baking tray
3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Bake for about 25 minutes.
5. CookBlend the veggies and 8 garlic cloves.
6. Add tofu, a splash of olive oil, a handful of fresh parsley, 3 tablespoons of nutritional yeast, 1/3 cup of raw almonds, and the juice of one lemon.
7. Season again with a bit of salt and pepper to taste.
8. Add vegetable broth to help blend everything to a smooth consistency. You can adjust the amount to make the sauce as thick or thin as you like.
9. Use the sauce with cooked chickpeas, drizzled over yogurt, mixed with broccoli pesto, or as a creamy pasta sauce.
10. For a gourmet touch, sprinkle toasted almond flakes and vegan parmesan on top before serving.
