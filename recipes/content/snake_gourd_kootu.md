# Snake Gourd Kootu

### Ingredients
- 2cupsSnake gourd
- ¼cup Chana dal
- 2 tablespoon Moong dal
- Turmeric
- Salt
- Oil
- Mustard seeds
- Urad Dal
- Curry leaves

### Instructions
1. Soak chana dal in water for 1 hour.
2. Clean and cut the snake gourd to your desired size cubes. I chopped little fine.
3. Take both the dals in a small pressure cooker and just add enough water to immerse the dal. Add turmeric and stir well.
4. Add the chopped veggie over it (don’t stir)
5. Pressure cook for 3 whistles in medium flame. Allow pressure to release naturally.
6. Add kootu powder and salt and bring to boil for a few minutes.
7. Add tempering items: mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves.
