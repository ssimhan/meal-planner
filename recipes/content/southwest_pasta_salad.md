# Southwest Pasta Salad

### Ingredients
- ½cup mayonnaise
- ¼cup sour cream
- Adobo seasoning
- Cilantro
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Chives
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Lime juice
- 1(12-ounce) box tri-color rotini pasta
- 1canblack beans
- 1½cups frozen fire-roasted corn
- 1 bell pepper, red
- 1/2 red onion
- 1cupcherry tomatoes, halved
- 1largeavocado, diced
- Cilantro
- Scallions

## Prep Steps
- Wash and chop herbs
- Chop peppers
- Chop onions/shallots
- Chop scallions

### Instructions
1. **Prep**Dice bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, scallionsCut cherry tomatoes, avocado
2. CookDrain and rinse black beans
3. Cook corn
4. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, chipotle peppers, minced cilantro, garlic powder, onion powder, chives, parsley, oregano, and lime juice. Set aside.
5. Cook the rotini according to the package instructions. Then, drain, rinse with cold water, and allow to dry for about 5 minutes.
6. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, black beans, corn, bell pepper, red onion, grape tomatoes, avocado, and chopped cilantro.
7. Pour in the chipotle dressing, and stir to fully coat the salad ingredients.
8. Add salt and pepper to taste.
9. Cover and place in the refrigerator, and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
10. Garnish with sliced scallions.
