# Spinach and Feta Stuffed Pita with a Side of Greek Salad

### Prep Steps

<!-- List prep tasks here -->

### Ingredients
- 2cupsspinach
- Feta cheese
- 1/2 red onion
- Garlic cloves
- 3 whole wheat pitas
- 1 medium cucumber
- 2 medium tomatoes
- Kalamata olives
- Lemon juice
- Dried oregano

### Instructions
1. Prep:Roughly chop 2 cups of fresh spinach.
2. Combine the chopped spinach with 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese, 1/4 cup of finely chopped onions, 1 minced garlic clove, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well.
3. Cut 2-3 whole wheat pitas in half, creating pockets.
4. CookCarefully spoon the spinach and feta mixture into each pita pocket, being careful not to overfill.
5. In a large skillet or grill pan over medium heat, add a little olive oil. Cook the stuffed pitas for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the outsides are golden and the filling is warmed through.
6. Greek Salad Prep:Dice 1 cucumber, 2 tomatoes, and 1/4 of a red onion. Place them in a bowl.
7. Add 1/4 cup of kalamata olives and 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese to the vegetables.
8. In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
9. Combine and serve
