# Spinach-Cilantro Pesto

### Ingredients
- 1clovegarlic
- 1/3cupalmonds
- 1cupfresh spinach
- 1bunchfresh cilantro
- 2tbspsherry vinegar
- 1/2cupolive oil
- 1tspsalt

### Instructions
1. Soak almonds to remove the skin.
2. In food processor, process the garlic and shallot. Once these are minced, add the almonds.
3. Next add the spinach, cilantro and sherry vinegar and continue to process until smooth.
4. Slowly add the olive oil until a thick pesto is formed, then salt to taste.
