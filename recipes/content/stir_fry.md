# Stir Fry

### Ingredients
- 16oztofu
- Garlic
- 1 package of green onions
- Ginger
- Soy sauce
- Rice vinegar
- Brown sugar
- Broth
- Cornstarch
- Black pepper or ketchup (optional)
- Sesame oil
- 1 onion
- 2 bell peppers, green
- Broccoli
- Frozen peas or corn
- Baby spinach (optional)
- Bean sprouts (optional)
- Baby corn (optional)
- Sugar snap peas (optional)

### Instructions
1. Check the infographic here: https://www.cooksmarts.com/articles/guide-to-stir-frying/
2. Choose a protein (1 pound)Tofu: cubed, extra firmSeiten: choppedTempeh: chopped
3. Choose aromatics (1 tablespoon)Garlic, shallots, green onions, ginger
4. Prep vegetables (4 cups)Onions - thinly slicedBell peppers - slicedCarrots - thinly slicedBroccoli - chopped and microwaved with damp paper towel for 2 minutesBaby spinachFrozen peas, edamame or cornSugar snap peas, trimmed and left wholeBean sproutsMushrooms - slicedGreen beans - trimmedBaby corn - drained and rinsed
5. Make the sauceOptions listed in infographic: https://www.cooksmarts.com/articles/guide-to-stir-frying/
6. Make the stir fryHeat a wok over medium high heat.
7. Once hot, add 2 tbsp sesame oil and protein and let sit for 4 minutes before tossing to coat in oil.
8. Saute until tofu is golden, remove from pan and set aside.
9. Wipe wok with paper towel if needed, return to medium-high heat.
10. Add cooking oil and aromatics (garlic, shallots, green onion, ginger).
11. Once you can smell them, add veggies to heated oil with sprinkle of salt.
12. Stir fry for 10 minutes until tender but still crunchy. If pan ever looks too dry, add splash of water or broth
13. Add protein back to wok, push all ingredients to sides forming a hole int he middle.
14. Place sauce in the hole, wait for it to bubble and darken before tossing everything through and making sure ll the ingredients are coated.
15. Cook until all ingredients are tender, then remove from heat.
16. Garnish with lemon juice, salt, cilantro, basil, green onion, cashews or peanuts.
