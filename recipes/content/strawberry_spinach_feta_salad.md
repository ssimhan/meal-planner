# Strawberry Spinach Feta Salad

### Ingredients
- 3cupshalved strawberries (about 1 pound)
- ½cup crumbled feta (about 4 ounces)
- ½red onion, thinly sliced
- ¾cup pecans, chopped
- 1cupdiced cucumber
- 5 ounces spinach
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon dijon
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- ¼cup balsamic vinegar
- ¾cup olive oil
- ½teaspoon salt
- ⅛teaspoon black pepper

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Slice cucumber
- Wash and chop greens
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. Add all of the ingredients for the salad to a large bowl.
2. In a small bowl or jar, mix together the dressing ingredients.
3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss. Start by using half of the dressing and then add more depending on personal preference.
