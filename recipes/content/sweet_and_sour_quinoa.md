# Sweet and Sour Quinoa

### Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon Oil
- 1 Red Onion ,finely chopped
- 2 Garlic Cloves ,minced
- 1 teaspoon Minced Ginger
- 2 tablespoon White Wine Vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Paste
- 1/2cuppineapple juice
- ½cup Water
- ½cup Quinoa
- 1 Egg (optional)

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Mince garlic
- Grate/mince ginger

### Instructions
1. Heat oil in pan over medium / high heat. Reduce heat and gently fry the onion until softened (approx. 5 mins). Add the garlic and ginger and fry for a further min.
2. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, tomato paste, pineapple juice, water and quinoa and then stir to combine.
3. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, cover and allow to simmer for 15 mins, until the liquid has been absorbed.
4. Turn the heat off, fluff with a fork, replace lid and leave the quinoa to sit for a further 5 mins.
5. Stir in the chopped pineapple and serve
