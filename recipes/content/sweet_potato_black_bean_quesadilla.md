# Sweet Potato Black Bean Quesadilla

## Ingredients
- 1 large sweet potato
- 1/2 cup frozen corn (or canned corn, drained)
- 1/2 cup cooked black beans (or canned, rinsed and drained)
- 1/2 cup grated cheese (like mozzarella or cheddar)
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
- Salt to taste
- 4 medium flour tortillas
- 1 tablespoon oil or ghee (for cooking)
- Optional: finely chopped onion, capsicum, green chili, or coriander leaves

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
