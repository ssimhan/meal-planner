# Tandoori Veggie Quesadillas

### Ingredients
- 1 bell pepper, red
- 1 bell pepper, green
- 1 onion, red
- 1tspcumin seeds
- 1tbspgarlic, minced
- 1tspgaram masala
- 1tsptaco seasoning
- 1tsppaprika
- 1tspred chili
- 1 pinch cinnamon, optional
- 1/4cupcilantro
- 1cupfrozen corn, rinsed and thawed
- Salt to taste
- 2tbspoil
- 1 package shredded mexican blend cheese
- 1 package tortillas
- 1cupcream cheese or Greek yogurt
- 2tspsriracha sauce

### Instructions
1. Set corn to cook in microwave.
2. Dice peppers and onion.
3. Set up rotimatic if using. Adults need 3-4, kids need 2-3.
4. Heat a large wide skillet over medium high heat. Add oil. Once oil is hot add the cumin seeds and let splutter for about 10-15 seconds.
5. Add the sliced peppers and onions and saut√© until slightly softened (about 4-5 minutes)
6. Drain the cooked corn and let it cool.
7. Add garlic, dry seasonings, and salt to the pan - reduce heat to low so the spices do not burn.
8. Add corn, cilantro and stir well. Taste test for spice and salt levels. Remove from heat.
9. Mix cream cheese and Sriracha sauce.
10. In a large pan heat up a little bit of oil and add one tortilla spread with a layer of the cream cheese mixture.
11. Top with cheese, a few spoonfuls of the veggie filling, additional cheese and fold the tortilla over.
12. Press in panini/sandwich maker for 1 minute. Remove from heat, cut and serve hot!
