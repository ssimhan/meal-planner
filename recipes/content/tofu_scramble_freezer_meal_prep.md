# Tofu Scramble (Freezer Meal Prep)

### Ingredients
- 1 package tofu
- 1candiced tomatoes
- 1 medium onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1/4cupbroth
- 2tbspsoy sauce
- 1tspDijon mustard
- 1/4cupnutritional yeast
- 5tspground turmeric
- 1tspgarlic salt
- 1tbspmaple syrup
- 1cupspinach
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Prep Steps
- Press/cube tofu
- Chop onions/shallots
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. Meal Prep Instructions:Label your gallon-sized freezer bag with the name, instructions, and a use-by date (recommended 90 days after prep date).Add all ingredients (except for the maple syrup and fresh kale/spinach) to the freezer bag.Stir gently to combine the ingredients.Remove air, seal the bag, and freeze.Cooking Instructions:
2. Instant Pot Instructions:
3. Thaw the freezer meal overnight in the fridge.From thawed, stir to combine and then pressure cook on high for 3 minutes. Use the Quick-release function after cooking.Open the Instant Pot and add the maple syrup and fresh kale or spinach. Stir to combine.Add salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste.
4. Serving Tips:Serve with fresh greens, toast, or sliced tomatoes with chives.
