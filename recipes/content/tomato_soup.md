# Tomato Soup

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs tomatoes, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until soft
2. Add tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes
3. Add broth and simmer for 20 minutes
4. Blend until smooth
5. Stir in cream and season with salt and pepper
6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot
