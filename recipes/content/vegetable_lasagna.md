# Vegetable Lasagna

### Ingredients
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 medium zucchini
- 1 bell pepper, red
- 1 medium onion
- 1 tablespoon garlic
- 1cupspinach
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons parsley
- 2tbspbasil
- 15 ounces ricotta cheese
- 2¼cups grated mozzarella cheese
- ½cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 24 ounces marinara sauce
- No-boil lasagna noodles

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Spray a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with cooking spray and set aside.
2. Dice zucchini, bell pepper, onion and spinach.
3. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat. Sauté veggies and garlic.
4. Use a separate bowl to stir together egg, parsley, basil, ricotta cheese, 1 ¼ cups mozzarella, and ¼ cup Parmesan.
5. When the vegetables are done cooking, stir the marinara sauce into the skillet.
6. Spread ¼ of the marinara-vegetable sauce mixture in a thin layer in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
7. Create layer of no-boil lasagna noodles, ⅓ of the ricotta mixture, and another ¼ of the sauce mixture.
8. Repeat layers two more times. Finish with remaining 1 cup of mozzarella and ¼ cup of Parmesan cheese on top. Cover with aluminum foil.
9. Bake for 35 minutes.
10. Remove foil, and bake for another 5-10 minutes (until cheese on top is browned).
11. Let stand for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
