# Vegetarian Burrito Bowl with Avocado Crema

### Ingredients
- 1 head of cauliflower
- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 red bell pepper
- ½onion
- ⅓cup olive oil
- 2tbsplime juice
- ¼cup Taco seasoning
- 2cansblack beans, refried
- ½cup salsa
- 2cupsromaine lettuce
- Extra cilantro for serving
- 4cupscilantro-lime rice
- Avocado Crema
- 1 medium Avocado
- 1 Garlic clove, minced
- 1/2cupSour cream
- 1/2tspsalt
- 3tbspLime juice
- 1/4cupCilantro
- 1cupextra long grain rice or basmati rice
- 11/4 cups water
- 2tspolive oil
- 1tspsalt
- 1/4cupcilantro
- 3tspvegetable oil
- 3tbsplime juice

## Prep Steps
- Cut florets
- Chop peppers
- Wash and chop herbs
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. Make cilantro lime riceInstant Pot: 1 cup rice, 1 1/4 water, 1 tsp olive oil, salt
2. High pressure for 3 minutes, natural pressure release for 10 minutes. Total time should be roughly 20 minutes
3. Combine chopped cilantro, lime juice, and some more oil. Fluff rice with fork and toss the mix together.
4. Burrito BowlPreheat the oven to 400°F.
5. Chop cauliflower, bell peppers, onion. Drain black beans.
6. In a large bowl toss cauliflower, onion, and bell peppers with the taco seasoning, olive oil and lime juice then spread out on a large (rimmed) baking sheet.
7. Place on the center rack and bake for 30 minutes or until the cauliflower is tender with crispy edges.
8. While the vegetables are cooking prepare avocado crema by blending avocado, garlic, sour cream, salt, lime juice, cilantro until smooth. Also chop the lettuce.
9. In a small skillet combine black beans and salsa over medium heat and cook for 5 minutes, until warmed through.
10. Serve the roasted taco vegetables over cilantro rice, with the beans, lettuce and avocado crema.
