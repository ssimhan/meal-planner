# Vegetarian Chili

### Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion
- 2 medium sweet potatoes
- 2 bell peppers, red
- 4clovesgarlic
- Chili powder
- Chipotle chili powder
- Cumin powder
- Salt
- 21/2 cups vegetable broth
- 1cantomato sauce, 8 oz
- 1/2cupuncooked quinoa
- 1canblack beans
- 1cankidney beans
- 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
- Cilantro
- Shredded cheese
- Crushed tortilla chips
- Greek yogurt

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Peel and cube sweet potatoes
- Chop peppers
- Wash and chop herbs

### Instructions
1. Dice yellow onion, sweet potatoes, red bell peppers.
2. Once the oil is hot, add the onion and cook until beginning to soften, about 3 minutes.
3. Add the sweet potatoes, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, chipotle chili powder, cumin, and kosher salt.
4. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes, until the garlic is fragrant.
5. Add 2.5 cups of the vegetable broth and the quinoa.
6. Pour the tomato sauce on top (do not stir again).
7. Cover and seal the Instant Pot. Cook on manual (HIGH) pressure for 8 minutes. Immediately release the pressure. Carefully open the Instant Pot.
8. Turn the Instant Pot to OFF. Stir in the black beans, kidney beans, and sugar. Place the lid back on top and let stand 10 minutes to thicken. Uncover. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired. Serve hot, with any and all the toppings!
9. Vegetarian chili is good in the refrigerator for 5 days and in the freezer for 3 months. Let the leftovers cool completely, and store them in an airtight container.
10. Can serve with salad, cornbread or roasted veggies
