# Vegetarian Tortilla Soup

### Ingredients
- 1 medium onion
- 1 bell pepper, green
- 4 garlic cloves
- 215-ounce cans black beans
- Olive oil
- Oregano
- Cumin powder
- 28-ounce can fire roasted tomatoes
- 1½cups frozen corn
- Adobo powder
- 4cupsbroth
- Salt
- 4 radishes
- 1 lime
- 1bunchcilantro

## Prep Steps
- Chop onions/shallots
- Chop peppers
- Mince garlic

### Instructions
1. PrepPeel and dice the onion. Dice the green pepper. Peel and mince the garlic. Drain and rinse the beans.
2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil and sauté the onion until translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the green pepper and the garlic and sauté for 2 minutes.
4. Stir in the oregano and the cumin for 1 minute.
5. Add the tomatoes, beans, corn, adobo sauce, broth, and kosher salt.
6. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Prep the garnishes: Slice the radishes. Slice the lime into wedges.
8. Serve: To serve, ladle the soup into bowls and allow to cool to warm. Garnish with the tortilla strips, radishes, torn cilantro leaves, hot sauce, and plenty of lime juice.
