# Veggie Wrap

### Ingredients
- 4 flour tortillas
- 2/3cuphummus
- 1cupedamame
- 2cupsspinach
- 1-2 medium avocados
- 1largecarrot
- 1 medium cucumber
- Other veggie ideas: chopped tomato, corn, lettuce, beets, bell peppers
- Salt & pepper

### Instructions
1. Shred carrot and chop cucumber.
2. Spread hummus over tortilla.
3. Add edamame, spinach, avocado, carrot, cucumber, and any other desired vegetables.
4. Sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper, to taste.
5. Drizzle with a tiny bit of olive oil. Roll up your wrap and enjoy!
6. *I love using regular hummus or roasted red pepper!
7. **Other vegetable ideas: tomato, radish, corn, lettuce, cabbage, red bell pepper, zucchini, alfalfa sprouts etc.
