# Zucchini and Paneer Waffles

### Ingredients
- 1 zucchini
- Salt
- Red chili powder
- Turmeric
- Amchur powder
- Shredded paneer
- Cilantro
- Olive oil
- Rice flour

### Instructions
1. Shred the Zucchini and add salt to it and leave it for 10 minutes
2. Drain and squeeze the water out of the Zucchini
3. Add chopped cilantro and shredded paneer
4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, amchur powder and salt only if needed
5. Add the rice flour and mix it well to form a thick batter (the longer the batter sits you may need more rice flour)
6. Grease the waffle maker with oil
7. Spread the batter on the waffle maker and cook for 10 minutes
8. Flip it mid way and cook on the other side
9. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve it along with Dill and Pepper Dip
