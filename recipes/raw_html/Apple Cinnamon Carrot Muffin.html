Apple Cinnamon Carrot Muffin

★★★★★

REW, School Snacks

Servings: 12 Source: Real Easy Weekdays

Ingredients

1 3/4 cup flour, wheat

1 1/2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp cinnamon

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt

2 red apples, medium

1 carrot, large


3/4 cup milk

2 eggs, large

1/2 cup honey or agave

1/2 cup avocado oil

1 tsp vanilla

Directions

Shred apples to get 1 1/2 cup for recipe, squeeze out extra moisture.
Shred carrot to get 1/2 cup for recipe, squeeze out extra moisture.

Preheat over to 350 degrees and prepare muffin tins

In a large mixing bowl, combine dry ingredients. Mix in apples and carrots until well combined.

In a small bowl, combine wet ingredients. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix to incorporate ingredients.

Spoon into muffin tins. Bake for approximately 16 minutes or until a
toothpick/knife inserted into the center comes out clean.

Notes

Substitute for one egg:
1 Tbsp chia seeds + 2.5 Tbsp water
1/4 cup yogurt
1/4 cup applesauce + 1/2 tsp baking powder
