Arisi Upma Mix

Indian, School Entrees

Ingredients

Basmati rice

Toor dal

Coconut oil

Mustard seeds

Urad dal

Cumin

Asoeftida

Water

Salt

Fresh coconut, optional

Directions

Soak one cup of basmati rice and 3-4 teaspoons of toor dal for 10 min

Wash it and strain the water completely

Towel dry for one hour

Grind it in Preethi mixer to coarse consistency and store in glass jar in fridge

When ready to use...

Heat coconut oil or canola+ghee mixed
Add mustard, urad dhal, jeera, asoeftida and wait for crackle

For every half cup of mix, add 1.5 cups of water.

Add salt and fresh coconut to taste

Keep stirring until cooked, adding water if needed
