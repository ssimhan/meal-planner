Bhel Puri Chaat

Dinner, Guest Apps, To Try Out

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 2 mins Servings: 2 servings Source: Hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

1 potato

1 Cucumber

1 Green apple, optional

1 Red onion

1 tomato

2 cup puffed rice

2 tbsp fried peanuts

½ tsp chaat masala

¼ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder

¼ tsp salt

3 tbsp tamarind chutney

2 tbsp green chutney

1 tsp lemon juice

2 tbsp sev

Cilantro

Directions

Roast peanuts.

Chop up veggies.

Add spices and mix well.

Add chutneys, lemon juice and cilantro. Mix well to distribute.

Serve with puffed rice, sev and peanuts.
