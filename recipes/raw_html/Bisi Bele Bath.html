Bisi Bele Bath

Indian

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 50 mins Servings: 4 servings Source: Hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

1 cup toor dal

2 cups rice

1 onion

2 tomatoes

2 carrots

1 cup beans

½ cup green peas

2 small potatoes

2 tbsp tamarind pulp

4 tbsp Bisi Bele Bath powder

1 tsp turmeric

Salt

water

ghee

2 tsp mustard seeds

2 tsp cumin

Curry leaves

4 dried red chilies

2 tbsp cashews

Directions

This recipe is for 4 adult lunch servings

Prep:
Rinse 1 cup toor dal and 2 cups rice well.

Chop onions, tomatoes, carrots, beans, potatoes, and gather other ingredients.

Cook:
Set the Instant Pot to ‘Sauté’ mode. Add 2 tbsp ghee, then mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, dried red chilies, and cashews. Sauté for 30 seconds.

Add onions and tomatoes, sauté until soft.

Then add all chopped vegetables. Mix in turmeric, salt, and 4 tbsp Bisi Bele Bath powder. Stir for 30 seconds.

Add rinsed toor dal and rice, followed by 8 cups water and 2 tbsp tamarind pulp. Mix well.

Cancel ‘Sauté’ mode, close the lid, and set Instant Pot to ‘Pressure Cook’ on HIGH for 10 minutes.

Allow pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.

Open the lid, stir well, and adjust consistency by adding a little hot water if needed.
