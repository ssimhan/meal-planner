Blueberry Chocolate Chip Muffin

REW, School Snacks, To Try Out

Prep Time: 20 Cook Time: 15 Source: Real Easy Weekdays

Ingredients

1 cup wheat flour

1/2 cup buckwheat flour

1/4 cup other flour (wheat or all purpose)

1/2 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 cup sugar

1/3 cup avocado oil

2 eggs

2/3 cup milk

2 tsp vinegar

2 tsp vanilla

1 sweet potato

1 cup blueberries (frozen ok)

1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees and prep muffin tins

Mix milk with 2 tsp of vinegar and let it sit for a few minutes

Peel sweet potato and grate (only use 3/4 cup of shredded SP in recipe).

In a large mixing bowl, mix flours, baking power, baking soda, salt and
sugar in a large mixing bowl.

In a separate mixing bowl, mix avocado oil, eggs, milk mixture, vanilla and sweet potato until smooth.

Pour liquid ingredients into dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed. Do not over mix.

Add blueberries and chocolate chips.

Pour into muffin tins.

Bake for 16 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Cool before eating or freezing.
