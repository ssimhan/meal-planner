Blueberry Date Oatmeal

Breakfast, REW, To Try Out

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 7 Servings: 2 Source: REW

Ingredients

1 cup rolled oats

2 Tbs frozen riced cauliflower

1 1⁄2 cup milk

6 dates

2 Tbs coconut oil

1⁄2 cup frozen blueberries

Directions

In a microwave-safe bowl, mix oats, cauliflower, milk and dates.

Microwave on high for approximately 6 minutes. Alternately, cook on stovetop, stirring frequently.

Stir in blueberries and coconut oil before serving
