Carrot Cake Bars

Dessert, To Try Out, Weekend Only

Source: Instagram.com

Ingredients

1 banana

1 egg

1/4 cup peanut butter ⁣

1/4 cup maple syrup ⁣

1 carrot

1 cup oat flour

1/2 tsp baking powder ⁣

1/2 tsp cinnamon ⁣

1/4 cup white chocolate chips (optional)⁣

Directions

Grate carrot and mash a ripe banana.

Preheat oven 180c⁣

In a prepared loaf tin, mix together the wet ingredients (banana, egg, peanut butter, maple syrup, carrot) and then the remaining ingredients. Mix well.⁣

Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean ⁣
