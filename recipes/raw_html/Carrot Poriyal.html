Carrot Poriyal

Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Source: sharmispassions.com

Ingredients

3 carrots

1 onion

2 tablespoon coconut grated


Oil

Mustard seeds

Urad dal

3 curry leaves

1 green chili

Directions

Wash carrots, remove the outer skin, grate it and keep aside.

Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds, urad dal and let it splutter.

Add curry leaves and fry for a min.

Add green chillies and saute for 2mins.

Now add onion, required salt and saute well till it is transparent.

Then add grated carrots.

Saute till it shrinks mins then sprinkle little water and cook covered till it turns soft and raw smell leaves. You can omit water and cook covered too.

Once it becomes dry add coconut, give a quick stir and switch off.

Switch off and serve Carrot Poriyal with sambar rice or any variety rice.

Notes

In case you want to pack and wants more shelf life add coconut saute for a while then switch off.
