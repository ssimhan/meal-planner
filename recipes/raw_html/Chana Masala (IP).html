Chana Masala (IP)

Dinner, Indian, Instant Pot, To Edit

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 1 hr Servings: 4 Source: ministryofcurry.com

Ingredients

1 can chickpeas

Ghee

Cumin seeds

1 yellow onion

2 plum tomatoes

Ginger

Garlic

Coriander powder

Salt

Kashmiri red chili powder

Turmeric

Garam masala

½ cup cilantro

Directions

Chop 1 large onion and 2 tomatoes (or 1 can of diced tomatoes). Drain and rinse 2 cans of chickpeas.

Turn on the Instant Pot and select the 'Sauté' function.

Add 2 tablespoons of ghee and let it heat up.

Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.

Add the finely chopped onions to the Instant Pot and sauté until golden brown.

Add the minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies (if using).
Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Add the chopped tomatoes (or canned diced tomatoes) and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.

Stir in the ground coriander, ground cumin, turmeric powder, paprika, and chili powder. Cook for another 2 minutes.

Add the drained and rinsed chickpeas to the Instant Pot. Mix well to coat the chickpeas with the spice mixture.

Add about 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot. Stir well.

Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to 'Sealing'.

Select the 'Manual' or 'Pressure Cook' function and set the timer for 5 minutes.

Once the cooking cycle is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully turn the valve to 'Venting' to release any remaining pressure.

Open the Instant Pot and sprinkle 1 teaspoon of garam masala over the chana masala. Stir well and adjust salt to taste.
