Cherry Tomato Salad

Dinner, Salad, School Snacks

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: 4 people Source: Themom100.com

Ingredients

¼ cup olive oil

3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

2 teaspoons agave

½ teaspoon minced garlic


4 cups cherry tomatoes

¼ cup chopped fresh oregano

Directions

In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, agave, garlic, salt, and pepper.

Add the tomatoes and the oregano and toss to combine. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
