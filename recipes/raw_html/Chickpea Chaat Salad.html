Chickpea Chaat Salad

Guest Apps, Indian, Mexican, To Try Out

Prep Time: 15 minutes Servings: Servings: 4 calories: 255 Source: cookrepublic.com

Ingredients

1 can chickpeas

1 onion, red

1 bell pepper, green

1 cucumber

1 tomato

1 carrot

Cilantro

Mint


Olive oil

Sriracha

Lemon juice

Garlic

Cumin powder

Chaat masala

Tamarind paste

Salt


Peanuts

Directions

Grate carrot
Dice onion, bell pepper, cucumber, tomato
Chop and toast peanuts

Place chickpeas, onion, bell pepper, cucumber, tomato, carrot, red chilli, coriander and mint in a large bowl. Toss and mix well.

Prepare the dressing by mixing olive oil, sriracha, lemon juice, garlic, cumin powder, chaat masala, tamarind paste, salt.

When ready to serve, add the dressing to the salad in the bowl. Toss and mix well to coat everything evenly with the dressing.

Top with peanuts and extra cilantro
