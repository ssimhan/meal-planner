Chocolate Banana Lentil Muffins (REW)

REW, School Snacks, To Try Out

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 20 Servings: 12 Source: Real Easy Weekdays

Ingredients

2/3 cup rolled oats

2/3 cup wheat flour

1 tsp baking soda

1 tsp cinnamon

1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)



1/3 cup dry green lentils (1 cup cooked)

1/4 cup oil

2 bananas

1/4 cup sugar

1 tsp vanilla

1 egg

Directions

Cook lentils

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Blend oats, flour, baking soda, and cinnamon. Transfer to mixing bowl.

Puree lentils and oil. Add bananas, sugar, vanilla, and egg and process until smooth

Pour liquid mixture into dry mixture. Mix until just incorporated. (If adding chocolate chips to muffins, add here.)

Put 3 chocolate chips into the bottom of each muffin cup.

Scoop muffin batter into each muffin cup. Press a chocolate chip into the top.

Bake for approximately 20 minutes
