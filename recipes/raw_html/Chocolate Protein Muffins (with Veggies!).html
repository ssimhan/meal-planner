Chocolate Protein Muffins (with Veggies!)

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: 12 regular muffins or about 24 mini Source: yummytoddlerfood.com

Ingredients

US Customary Metric

1 cup peanut butter

1 cup banana

1 cup raw zucchini, or 2nd banana

1 cup spinach

3 eggs

¼ cup honey

Vanilla extract

¼ cup cocoa powder

2 tablespoons ground flaxseeds

1 teaspoon baking powder

¼ teaspoon baking soda

Chocolate chips

¼ teaspoon salt

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease the muffin tin.

Place all ingredients into a blender and blend until very smooth.

Fill muffin cups about ½ full using about ¼ cup batter in each. Top with chocolate chips, if using.

Bake 20 minutes for regular muffins and 12 for mini muffins.

Let cool for 5-10 minutes in the pan, run a knife around the edges, and carefully transfer to a wire rack to cool.
