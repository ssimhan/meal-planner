Chow Chow Kootu

Indian

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Servings 3 Source: rakskitchen.net

Ingredients

1 chow chow

¼ cup Moong dal

1 teaspoon Sambar powder

⅛ teaspoon Turmeric

Salt

¼ cup Coconut grated

½ teaspoon Cumin seeds

½ teaspoon Rice flour optional


Oil

Mustard seeds

Urad dal

Curry leaves

Directions

Peel and dice chow chow.

Add moong dal, turmeric, sambar powder and salt.

Add a cup of water and make sure everything is mixed together

Put in IP for 8 minutes manual mode and then natural pressure release. Full process should take 20 minutes.

Meanwhile, mix rice flour into a small amount of water.

Grind coconut with the rice flour mixture well.

Add a little jeera and grind coarsely.

(Finely chop small onions if you have time)

By now, pressure should have release, so add the mixture to cooked vegetables and bring to boil in saute mode.

Thalichify with coconut oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves. Add finely chopped small onion and mix well.
