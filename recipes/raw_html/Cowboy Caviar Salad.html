Cowboy Caviar Salad

★★★★

Salad

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Yield: 5 - 6 servings Source: Herwholesomekitchen.com

Ingredients

2 roma tomatoes, seeded and diced

1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed

2 cups corn kernels

½ red onion, diced

1 green bell pepper, diced

1 jalapeno, diced

1 cup black eyed peas, drained and rinsed

¼ chopped cup cilantro



⅓ cup olive oil

2 tablespoons white wine vinegar

1 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup)

1 lime, juiced

½ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

In a small bowl, mix together the dressing ingredients.

Cook corn in microwave.

Add all of the salad ingredients to a large bowl and drizzle the dressing on top.

Enjoy! Serve plain or as a dip for tortilla chips. Optionally, refrigerate 10 - 15 minutes before serving to let the flavors marinate.
