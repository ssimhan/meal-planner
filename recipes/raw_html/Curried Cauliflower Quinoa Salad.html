Curried Cauliflower Quinoa Salad

To Try Out

Prep Time: 40 min Cook Time: 40 min Servings: Serves: 4 Source: themodernproper.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup Greek yogurt

1 cucumber

Olive oil

1/3 cup tahini

Lemon juice

Garlic

Salt

1 small head cauliflower

Cilantro

2 cup quinoa

2 cup red cabbage

4 cup arugula

Directions

Preheat oven to 425Â°F.

Cook the quinoa. Dice all the cucumbers.

In a large bowl toss cauliflower with olive oil, desired spices (cumin, coriander, etc) and salt. Spread onto a baking sheet in a single layer and bake on center rack for 40 minutes.

While the cauliflower is cooking prepare the salad dressing by combining yogurt, tahini, cucumber, oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt in a blender and blending until smooth.

In a large bowl toss together cooked quinoa, red cabbage and arugula and divide between 4 bowls.

Top each bowl with fresh cilantro, cucumbers, golden raisins and roasted cauliflower.

Drizzle with yogurt tahini dressing and enjoy!
