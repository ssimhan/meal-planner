Date Cookies

★★★★

REW, School Snacks

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 15 Servings: 24 Source: Real Easy Weekdays

Ingredients

1 cup medjool dates

1/2 cup butter, melted

1 large egg

1 tsp vanilla

1/3 cup buckwheat flour

2/3 cup white whole wheat flour

1 cup oats

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp cinnamon

1/4 cup greek yogurt

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and place parchment paper on cookie sheets.

Put dates and butter in a food processor. Process until smooth.

Add egg, Greek yogurt and vanilla to the food processor. Mix thoroughly.

Take a new bowl and mix buckwheat flour, white whole wheat flour, oats, baking soda, and cinnamon.

Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and mix fully.

Use ice cream scooper to make small balls of dough. Place on greased cookie sheets and press down on the top.

Bake 12-15 min - until lightly brown.
