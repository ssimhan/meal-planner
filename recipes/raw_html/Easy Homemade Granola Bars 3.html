Easy Homemade Granola Bars

Breakfast, To Edit, To Try Out

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: 16 Source: Yummytoddlerfood.com

Ingredients

1½ cups rolled oats

½ cup shredded unsweetened coconut (or use ½ cup additional oats)

¼ cup agave

¼ cup whole-wheat flour

3 tablespoons peanut butter

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

½ teaspoon cinnamon

⅛ teaspoon baking powder

⅛ teaspoon salt

2 tablespoons chia seeds

¼ cup water

Description

### Simplified Granola Bar Recipe

#### Prep:
1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a Silpat.
2. If honey and peanut butter are not at room temperature, warm them for about 15 seconds.
3. Place oats and coconut in a food processor and blend until they resemble quick oats.
4. Add the remaining ingredients (peanut butter, honey, and any flavor variations) to the food processor and blend well.
5. Add ¼ cup of water and blend until the mixture is thick and sticks together when pressed. Add more water if needed.

#### Cook:
1. Pour the batter onto the prepared baking sheet and press it flat with your hands to about ¼-½ inch thick, forming a rough 8x8 inch square.
2. Score the batter lightly with a knife to make squares of your desired size.
3. Press chocolate chips into the top if desired.
4. Bake for 18-20 minutes.
5. Let the bars cool completely on the pan.
6. Break into squares and serve.

#### Storage:
- Fridge: Store in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks.
- Room Temperature: Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
- Freezer: Place cooled bars in a sealed zip-top freezer bag for up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in the fridge before serving.

#### Flavor Variations:
- Use fruit or veggie purees like applesauce, banana, butternut squash, sweet potato, or pumpkin instead of water.
- Add chocolate chips by pressing them into the batter before baking.

Enjoy your homemade granola bars!
