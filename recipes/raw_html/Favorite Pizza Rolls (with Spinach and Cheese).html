Favorite Pizza Rolls (with Spinach and Cheese)

Dinner, Italian

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 18 minutes Servings: 12 Source: Yummytoddlerfood.com

Ingredients

1 cup baby spinach

13-16 ounces pizza dough (at room temperature)

3/4 cup pizza sauce

2 cups shredded mozzarella

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

Prep:
Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.

Grease a muffin tin, making sure to coat the top edges of each cup (cheese may spill over).

Stretch the pizza dough into an 11x16-inch rectangle on a piece of parchment paper (this will prevent sticking). If the dough is too tough to stretch, let it rest for 5 minutes and try again.

Chop the spinach if it's not pre-chopped.

Cook:
Spread the Toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough. Sprinkle on the spinach, mozzarella, and Parmesan evenly.

Roll the Dough: Starting on one long side, roll the dough up into a log, using the parchment paper to help you roll.

Slice: Cut the rolled dough into 12 even slices using a serrated knife. To make it easier, start cutting in the center, then work on cutting each half evenly.

Bake: Place each slice into the greased muffin tin. Bake for 22-26 minutes, or until the dough is baked through, the cheese is melted, and the tops are golden brown.

Cool: Let the rolls cool in the muffin tin for 3-5 minutes to let the cheese firm up. Use a knife to loosen any stuck cheese before transferring them to a wire rack or serving plate.

Storage:
Fridge: Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
Freezer: Freeze in a zip-lock bag for up to 3 months. To reheat, microwave frozen rolls for 30-60 seconds or until warm.

Notes

TIP: Place the dough on parchment when forming it into a rectangle to avoid having it stick to the counter.
