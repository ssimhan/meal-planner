Granny Eggs

Breakfast, REW, To Try Out

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 10 min Servings: Serving size: 2 Source: aol.com

Ingredients

French, country or whole wheat bread

1 egg for each slice of bread

1 tbsp butter

Salt and pepper

Directions

Cut a circle in each slice of bread, so it has a large hole in it.

Over medium heat, heat greased skillet.

Put bread on the skillet and crack an egg into the hole. Break yolk, if desired.

Once the egg is mostly cooked, flip bread and cook through.

Serve with the circle as a piece of plain bread or toast.
