Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup

Dinner, Sandwich, Soup

Ingredients

Wheat bread


Cheese, sliced or shredded


Butter


Garlic powder (optional)


2 cartons Trader Joe's tomato soup


1/2 cup milk

Directions

Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each slice of bread.

(Optional) Sprinkle a pinch of garlic powde inside the sandwich for added flavor.

Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on the preheated pan.

Add the cheese slices on top, along with a slice of tomato if desired, then cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.

Cook on medium heat for about 2-3 minutes on one side, pressing gently with a spatula, until golden brown.

Flip carefully and cook the other side for another 2-3 minutes until the bread is crispy and the cheese is fully melted.

Pour the Trader Joe's tomato soup into a small pot or microwave-safe bowl.

Heat on the stovetop for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, or microwave for 2-3 minutes.

For a creamier soup, stir in a splash of milk or cream while heating.

Cut the grilled cheese in half (diagonally for a classic touch!).

Pour the hot tomato soup into a bowl.

Pair your sandwich with the soup and enjoy dipping for extra flavor.
