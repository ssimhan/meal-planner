Guacamole

★★★★★

Guest Apps, Mexican, School Snacks

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: Servings 2 to 4 servings Source: Simplyrecipes.com

Ingredients

2 ripe avocados

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

1 tablespoon lemon juice

2 to 4 tablespoons minced red onion

2 tablespoons cilantro, finely chopped

Pinch freshly ground black pepper

1/2 ripe tomato, chopped

Directions

Cut the avocados, score and mash in the bowl.

Sprinkle with salt and lime (or lemon) juice.

Add the chopped onion, cilantro, black pepper.

If making a few hours ahead, place plastic wrap on the surface of the guacamole and press down to cover it to prevent air reaching it. (The oxygen in the air causes oxidation which will turn the guacamole brown.)

Refrigerate leftover guacamole up to 3 days.

Notes

Note: Chilling tomatoes hurts their flavor. So, if you want to add chopped tomato to your guacamole, add it just before serving.
