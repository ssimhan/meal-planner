Healthy Vegetable Sheet Pan Frittata

Dinner, To Try Out

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 6 Source: Runninginaskirt.com

Ingredients

1 teaspoon olive oil

½ cup onion, chopped

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

3 cups baby spinach, loosely packed

¼ teaspoon garlic powder

12 eggs

2 tablespoons milk

¼ cup ricotta

2 teaspoons italian seasoning

2 tablespoons feta

salt and pepper

cooking spray

Directions

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees and put a quarter sheet pan (9x13 inches) in the oven.

In a large saute pan heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and saute a few minutes until starting to turn tender. Add the tomato and cook another couple of minutes until they start to break. Add the spinach and cook just until it starts to wilt. Generously sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

While that's cooking whisk together the eggs, milk, ricotta and italliann seasoning. Then salt and pepper the mixture. I used ½ teaspon of each.

Remove the sheet pan from the oven and spray with cooking spray. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and carefully spoon the veggie mixture on top. Sprinkle with the feta cheese.

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the eggs are cooked.

Notes

Serve with bread or a salad! If you cut this into 8 portions it will be 135 calories per serving which would be wonderful for a breakfast sandwich.
