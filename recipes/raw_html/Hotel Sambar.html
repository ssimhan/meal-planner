Hotel Sambar

Dinner, Hebbar Kitchen, Indian

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: 4 Source: hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

1½ cups toor dal, cooked

1 cup tamarind extract

Turmeric powder

1 teaspoon jaggery

2 green chilies

Curry leaves

½ onion

5 green beans

1 carrot

1 tomato

Salt

Water

Sambar powder

Cilantro


For Tempering:

Oil

Mustard seeds

Dried red chili

Curry leaves


Directions

**Prep:
**
Cook 1/2 cup of toor dal.

Chop carrot, potato, tomato, onion.

Soak tamarind: Take a small lemon-sized piece of tamarind and soak it in ¼ cup warm water for about 10 minutes. Then squeeze and strain to get the juice.

Cook:
Take the chopped carrot, potato, and onion with 1 cup water.
Boil until vegetables are soft and cooked through.

Add the chopped tomato to the cooked vegetables and cook for 2 minutes.

Add the mashed dal to the pot.

Stir in 1½ tablespoons sambar powder.

Add the tamarind extract and salt to taste.

Let everything simmer together for about 5 minutes.

Make the tempering (tadka):

In a small pan, heat 2 teaspoons oil.

Add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds and let them pop.

Add a pinch of asafoetida and 1 dried red chili, broken in half.

Let it sizzle for a few seconds, then turn off the heat.

Pour the tempering into the simmering sambar.

Mix well and let it simmer 2 more minutes.

Add cilantro for garnish.
