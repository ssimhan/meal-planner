IP No Soak Kidney Beans / Rajma

★★★★★

Instant Pot

Prep Time: 23 minutes Cook Time: 45 min Total Time: 1 hr 15 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Servings (3/4-cup servings) Source: Minimalistbaker.com

Ingredients

1 cup kidney beans

3 ½ cups water

Directions

Add kidney beans and water to the Instant Pot and stir to prevent sticking.

Pressure cook on high (40 minutes for more firm, 45 for more tender)

It take about 8 minutes for the Instant Pot to pressurize before cooking begins.

Allow to naturally release for 15 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

Carefully remove lid once steam has fully escaped and strain off any excess cooking liquid.

Store cooled leftovers in the refrigerator up to 5 days or in the freezer up to 1 month.

Reheat in a saucepan on the stovetop, adding a little water, as needed, to prevent sticking.

Notes

3/4 cup dry beans= 1.75 cups cooked = 15oz canned kidney beans

*Total time includes active cook time, release time, and the time it takes for the Instant Pot to heat up (~8 minutes).
