IP Quinoa Chili

★★★★★

Dinner, Instant Pot, Mexican, Soup

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: Servings: 4 Source: pipingpotcurry.com

Ingredients

1 onion

1 bell pepper, green

3 cloves garlic

2/3 cup quinoa

14.5 ounce can Fire-roasted diced Tomatoes

1 can black beans

1 cup frozen corn

Red chili powder

Paprika

Cumin powder

Salt

2 cup broth


Cilantro

Lemon wedges

Sour cream

Directions

Prep:
Dice 1 cup yellow onion.
Dice 1 cup green bell pepper.
Finely dice ½ a jalapeño.
Mince 3 cloves garlic.
Wash ⅓ cup quinoa.
Rinse and drain 1 can black beans (about 1.25 cups).

Cook

Add all the ingredients to the Instant Pot in this order:
Diced onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, and garlic.

Washed quinoa, 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), 1 cup frozen corn, and the rinsed black beans.

Add ¾ tablespoon chili powder, ½ tablespoon paprika, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, and 1 teaspoon salt (adjust later to taste).

Pour in 2 cups vegetable broth.

Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the vent to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.

Release Pressure: Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally (this might take 10-15 minutes). Open the lid and taste the chili. Adjust the salt if necessary.

Serve: Garnish with chopped cilantro. Add any optional toppings you like:

Shredded cheddar
Avocado, lemon wedges, or diced red onion
Sour cream or greek yogurt
Serve with tortilla chips

Refrigerate: Store in an airtight container for up to 4 days.
Freeze: Portion into small containers and freeze. Reheat in the microwave or on the stove when ready to eat.
