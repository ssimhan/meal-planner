Idli Dosa Batter

Indian

Source: Cookwithrenu.com

Ingredients

1 Cup Urad Dal

3 Cups Idli Rice

1/4 tablespoon Salt

1 tablespoon fenugreek

1/2 tsp baking soda or baking powder

Directions

Rinse the Urad Dal and methi in water for 2-3 times.

Rinse the rice separately 2-3 times.

Soak the Urad dal and methi in one bowl, and Rice in another bowl for minimum 4 hours but ideally overnight.

Preheat oven to 200F.

Grind the Urad dal with minimum water to fine and fluffy consistency.

Grind the rice with minimum water.

Add 1/4 tablespoon of salt and mix everything with hand for at least 2-3 minutes.

Add 1/4 to 1/2 tsp. of baking soda (or baking powder) while you mix the batter if it's a very cold day.

Turn off oven and place batter for 10-24 hours.
