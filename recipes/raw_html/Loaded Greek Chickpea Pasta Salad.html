Loaded Greek Chickpea Pasta Salad

★★★★★

Dinner, Middle Eastern, Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: Yield: 4-6 Source: shelikesfood.com

Ingredients

8 ounce box of pasta

1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas

1 red bell pepper

1 yellow bell pepper

1 1/2 cups cherry tomatoes

1 cucumber, large diced

1 cup feta, crumbled

3/4 cup kalamata olives

Parsley


1/3 cup olive oil

2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

1 clove garlic

1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard

1/2 teaspoon maple syrup

1/4 teaspoon oregano

Salt

Black pepper

Directions

Prep

Dice bell peppers and cucumber.

Halve cherry tomatoes.

Chop kalamata olives


Cook
Prepare all vegetables and place them in a large bowl with the feta, chickpeas and parsley.

Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and add to the bowl.

Add all dressing ingredients to a small bowl and whisk until combined. This includes olive oil, wine vinegar, garlic, dijon mustard, maple syrup, oregano, salt and pepper.

Pour dressing over pasta salad and mix all ingredients together until it’s combined.

Pasta salad can be served immediately or refrigerated up to 3 days.

If turning into Mason jar salad, layer as
dressing, pasta, veggies, chickpeas, feta.
