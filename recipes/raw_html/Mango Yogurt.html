Mango Yogurt

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: 2 Source: Yummytoddlerfood.com

Ingredients

1 cup fresh or frozen mango

1/2 cup Mango Puree

1 cup greek yogurt

1 teaspoon fresh lime juice

1/2 teaspoon fresh lime zest

Directions

If making with fresh mango: Add fruit to a blender and blend smooth.

If making with frozen mango: Thaw at room temperature or warm for about 1 minute in the microwave. Add them to the blender and blend smooth.

If making with mango puree, add directly.

Stir together with the yogurt to make a uniform mixture.

Add any optional ingredients (lime juice, lime zest)

Stir and serve with granola.

Notes

Store in airtight containers or reusable pouches for 3-5 days in the refrigerator.
