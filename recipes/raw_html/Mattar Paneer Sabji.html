Mattar Paneer Sabji

Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: 5 servings Source: Hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

2 tbsp oil

2 onions

4 cloves garlic

4 tomatoes

2 tbsp cashew

Oil

Ghee

1 tsp cumin

2 green chili

Turmeric

Red chili powder

Coriander powder

Cumin powder

Garam masala

3 cup water

Salt

1½ cup peas

200 grams paneer

2 tbsp milk

1 tsp kasuri methi

Cilantro

Directions

**Prep:
**Roughly chop onion and tomatoes.
Cube paneer
Cook the frozen peas in the microwave.

Cook
Heat oil, ginger, garlic. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.

Add 4 tomatoes, 2 tbsp cashew, and saute until the tomato turn soft and mushy.

Cool completely, and transfer to a mixer jar.

Grind to smooth paste without adding water.

In the pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. add 1 tsp ghee, 1 tsp cumin, and 2 chilli. Saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, and ½ tsp garam masala.

Saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Add prepared gravy and cook until the oil separates from the gravy base.

Add 3 cup water and 1 tsp salt.

Add 1½ cups peas and paneer cubes.

Cover and cook for 5 minutes.

Add 2 tbsp cream, 1 tsp kasuri methi, and 2 tbsp coriander.

Serve with roti or rice.
