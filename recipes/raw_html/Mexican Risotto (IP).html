Mexican Risotto (IP)

★★★★★

Dinner, Instant Pot, Mexican, School Entrees

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 5 Source: instructables.com

Ingredients

2 cups arborio rice

Garlic

2 jalapenos

Cilantro

6 cups vegetable broth

1/2 red onion

Taco seasoning

1 packet chipotle corn from Trader Joe's

1/2 cup monterey jack cheese

Oil

Butter

Directions

Prep
Roughly chop the cilantro.
Grate the cheese.
Leave frozen corn out at room temp to defrost a bit.

Make

Saute the onions in the oil and butter.

After onions have started to caramelize, add the rice, garlic, and jalapeño. Stir occasionally until the rice is translucent (1 - 2 min).

Add broth, taco seasoning, spices and set the instant pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.

Heat up the entire packet of chipotle corn with sauce pellets in pan on stove.

Manually release the pressure when done. Add the corn, cheese and half the cilantro. Stir to combine.

Top with cilantro to eat.
