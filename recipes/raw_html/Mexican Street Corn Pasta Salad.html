Mexican Street Corn Pasta Salad

Mexican

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: Servings: 6 servings Source: Femalefoodie.com

Ingredients

2 cups small pasta

Olive oil

3 cups corn

2 cloves garlic

1/2 jalapeño, ribs removed

1 bunch cilantro

1 bunch scallions

1/4 cup cotija cheese, crumbled


1/2 cup sour cream

1/2 cup mayo

2 chipotle peppers in adobo

1/3 cup fresh lime juice

Salt

Black pepper

Directions

Prep:

Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.

Mince garlic, jalapeño, and chop cilantro and scallions (reserve some for garnish).

Crumble cotija cheese (reserve some for garnish).



Cook:

Heat olive oil in a skillet, add corn in a single layer, season with salt and pepper, and cook undisturbed for 5 minutes until charred.

Add garlic, cook for 1–2 more minutes, and cool.

Blend sour cream, mayonnaise, chipotle peppers, lime juice, and zest to make the dressing. Season with salt and pepper.



Assemble:

Combine pasta, charred corn, jalapeño, cilantro, scallions, and cotija cheese in a large bowl.

Toss with the dressing and adjust seasoning if needed.

Garnish with reserved cilantro, scallions, and cotija cheese. Serve or refrigerate.
