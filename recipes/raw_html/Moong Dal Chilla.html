Moong Dal Chilla

★★

Dinner, Indian, To Edit

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: Servings: 4 Source: pipingpotcurry.com

Ingredients

1 cup Moong Dal

2 Green Chili

1 teaspoon Ginger

1 onion

1 carrot

Paneer, optional


Cilantro

Salt

1 teaspoon turmeric

½ teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder

Water

Oil

Directions

Add moong dal to a medium bowl. Rinse the moong dal and soak for 3+ hours (ideally overnight).

If desired, chop onion, grate carrot and paneer.

Drain the water. Transfer dal to a blender. Add about ⅔ cup water. Blend to make a smooth batter.

Transfer the batter to a bowl. Add green chili, ginger, onion, carrot, cilantro, salt, turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well, and add 1-2 tablespoons more water if needed. Make sure the batter is not too thick or too runny, it should have a pouring consistency.

Make chilla like you would make dosa, adding grated paneer when semi-solid.

Serve right way with chutney or tomato ketchup, and a cup of chai!
