Palak Paneer Spinach

Dinner, Indian, School Entrees

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Source: hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

1 bunch spinach

2 cloves garlic

Ginger

3 green chillies


Oil

Butter

1 dried red chilli

Cumin powder

Bay leaf

1 onion

Ginger garlic paste

Turmeric

Red chili powder

Coriander powder

Salt

1 bell pepper, green

1 tomato

1 cup water

1 tbsp milk

250 grams paneer

Garam masala

Kasuri methi

Directions

Prep
Cut the onion, bell pepper, tomato.
Cube the paneer

Cook
Prep the ice cold water in a bowl to the side.

Heat up water in a large pot until it comes to rolling boil.

Turn off the flame and drop in the spinach.

Let it sit in the hot water for 2 minutes or until the leaves are blanched well.

After the 2 minutes are up, drop the palak leaves into ice-cold water. This helps to retain bright green colour.

Now transfer the blanched palak into the mixie.

Add 2 cloves garlic, ginger, 3 green chilies.

Blend to smooth puree without adding any water, and keep aside.

In a large kadai, heat 2 tbsp oil, 1 tsp butter, 1 dried red chilli, 1 tsp cumin powder and 1 bay leaf. Saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.

Add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute until onions turn golden brown.

Still on low, add turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder and salt. Saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Add the bell pepper and sauté until the capsicum shrinks slightly.

Add 1 tomato and cook until the tomato turns soft and mushy.

Add in prepared palak puree and cook for 5-10 minutes

Mix cream/milk and crumbed paneer. Sauce the cubed paneer with spices as desired.

Add 1 cup water to the spinach puree and adjust the consistency as required.

Add the milk/paneer into the mix and stir well making sure the consistency is appropriate.

Add cubed paneer and simmer for 2 minutes.

Add ½ tsp garam masala and 1 tsp kasuri methi

Enjoy with chapati or rice.
