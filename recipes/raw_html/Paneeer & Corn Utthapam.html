Paneeer & Corn Utthapam

Indian

Source: instagram.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup Rava

1/4 cup curd

1/4 cup water

Salt to taste

Red chili powder or chili flakes

1/2 tsp cumin powder

1/2 tsp pizza seasonings

Handful paneer cubes diced small

1/4 cup boiled corn

1/2 onion chopped

1 tbsp coriander leaves

Directions

Mix all and keep aside for 10 minutes, add a spoon or two water if needed

Cut 2 large capsicum into little thick slices

Take a non stick or a cast iron pan, spread oil once heats, layer the rings fill with uttapam batter properly

Let cook in low medium flame until browns, flip and cook the other side as well. It should brown properly

Enjoy with ketchup or chutney
