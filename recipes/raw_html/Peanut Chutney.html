Peanut Chutney

Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Servings 4 Source: rakskitchen.net

1 curry leaf

Directions

Dry roast peanuts in low flame to ensure even roasting.

Once its is crisp, transfer to a plate for cooling and roast channa and urad dal with a teaspoon of oil.

Lastly add green chillies and fry.

Cool everything.

Remove the skin of the peanuts completely.

Place in the mixer and grind with ginger (if desired), salt and water. Grind smoothly.

Adjust water consistency to make it runny.

Temper: oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaf.

Mix well.

Notes

I always make the kadai hot and add peanuts, roast in low flame for more time to ensure even roasting. As it gets roasted, it slightly splits.

If you want, you can add coconut in this same recipe.
