Pesarattu

Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 4 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: 14 + Source: indianhealthyrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 cup whole moong beans

Ginger paste

2 green chilies

Cumin seeds

Salt

Ghee


2 onions

2 green chilies (chopped)

Directions

Rinse well a few times until the water runs clear.

Pour fresh water and soak in enough water for about 4 to 6 hours. If using rice soak it along with dal. I don’t use rice most of the times.

Once the green gram is soaked, rinse them well.

Blend with ginger, salt, green chilies, cumin seeds.

Chop onions. Sauté with ghee, cumin, green chilies until slightly brown

When making the peasrattu, sprinkle fried onions on topping

To make the next one, ensure the pan is not very hot. If it is too hot, reduce the flame or turn off the stove for a while and then make the next one.

If you are unable to spread the batter on the tawa it means the batter is very thick. Add back to the blender with little water and blend again. Adding water directly to the batter most times doesn’t yield me good ones.
