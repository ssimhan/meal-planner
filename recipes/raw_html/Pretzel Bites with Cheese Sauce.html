Pretzel Bites with Cheese Sauce

Guest Apps, School Snacks

Total Time: 1 hour Source: Food Explorers

Ingredients

1 Tbsp Butter

1 Tbsp Flour

1 cup Whole Milk

1 cup Cheddar Cheese shredded

1/2 tsp Salt


3/4 cup lukewarm water (100°F – 110°F)

1/2 tablespoon sugar

1 tsp instant or active dry yeast

2 cups flour

1/2 teaspoon salt

Olive oil

1/4 cup baking soda

Directions

**Pretzel Bite- Instructions
**

Preheat the oven to 450 degree F. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper

Warm water in a pot until lukewarm. Add it to yeast and whisk.

Sprinkle sugar in it and whisk again. Set aside to let the yeast “bloom.”

Measure flour, salt, and add to a mixing bowl with yeast mix.

If dough is too dry, add more water 1 tablespoon at a time and if the dough is too wet, add more flour 1 tablespoon at a time. Place dough in a lightly oiled large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise to minimum 10 min

Pour 6 cups of water into the pot and add baking soda. Bring to a boil over high heat.

Divide the dough into 3 pieces. Roll each piece into a 20 to 24-inch long rope. Cut into bite-size pieces.

Using a slotted spoon, gently place the bite size pieces into the boiling water for 45 seconds. Remove the pretzels with a slotted spoon and drain well. Place onto the parchment paper-lined baking sheet.

Melt butter in the oven. Generously brush with melted butter on all sides. Sprinkle with coarse salt.

Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the pretzels are golden brown. While the preServe with Sauce for dipping!


**Cheese sauce
**
In a small saucepan set over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour and cook for 30 seconds, whisking constantly.

Slowly whisk in the milk until no lumps of flour remain.

Stirring constantly, bring the milk to a simmer over medium-low heat until it thickens, about 3 to 5 minutes.

Remove the pan from the stove and stir in the shredded cheese until all of the cheese has melted.

Add a pinch of salt, if needed. Serve warm.
