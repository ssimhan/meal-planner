Rajma Nachos

★★★★★

Dinner, Indian, Instant Pot

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 45 mins Servings: Servings: 4 Source: pipingpotcurry.com

Ingredients

1 cup Red Kidney Beans

1 tablespoon Ghee

1 teaspoon cumin

Ginger

Garlic

1 Green chili

2 Onion

4 Tomatoes

1 tablespoon Lime juice

Cilantro

½ teaspoon Turmeric

1 teaspoon Cumin powder

1 teaspoon Coriander powder

½ teaspoon Garam Masala

½ teaspoon Red Chili powder

Greek yogurt

Tomatoes (optional)

Salsa (optional)

Guacamole (optional)

8 oz shredded cheese

Nacho chips

Directions

Start the instant pot in Saute (More) mode and let it heat until it displays HOT. Add oil, cumin seeds and green chili.

Add onions, ginger and garlic. Stir and saute for about 3 minutes.

Add tomatoes and spices to the instant pot. Stir and saute for 2 more minutes.

Drain the old soaking water from the soaked beans and discard. Add the beans and the water (for cooking) to the instant pot. Stir it all up.

Close the lid with vent in sealing position. Change the setting to "Bean/Chili" mode. This will set the timer to 30 minutes at high pressure cooking. Once the instant pot beeps, let the pressure release naturally and then open the lid. If using canned beans, use 6 min instead of 30 and reduce water to 1 cup.

Add lime juice and cilantro and stir the rajma.

Serve on top of chips with cheese, greek yogurt/sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, salsa as toppings.
