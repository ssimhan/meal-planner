Rasam Recipe

Dinner, Hebbar Kitchen, Indian

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: 3 Source: hebbarskitchen.com

Ingredients

2 tomatoes

½ cup toor dal

Water

Tamarind concentrate

1–2 green chilies

1 tsp jaggery

1 tsp rasam powder

Salt

Oil

Mustard seeds

Cumin seeds

1 dried red chili

Hing

Curry leaves

Cilantro

Directions

Rinse ½ cup toor dal until the water runs clear.

Chop 2 medium tomatoes.

In a pressure cooker, add the rinsed dal, chopped tomatoes, 1½ cups water, 1 tsp tamarind concentrate, slit green chilies, 1 tsp jaggery, 1 tsp rasam powder, and salt.

Close the lid and cook on medium heat for 3 to 4 whistles.

Let the pressure release naturally. Open the lid and gently mash the dal and tomatoes.

In a small pan, heat 1 tsp ghee. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.

Add cumin seeds, dried red chili, a pinch of hing, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

Pour the tempering into the rasam. Stir to combine.

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
