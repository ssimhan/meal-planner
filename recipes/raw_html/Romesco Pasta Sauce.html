Romesco Pasta Sauce

Source: instagram.com

Ingredients

3 red peppers

2 tomatoes

8 cloves garlic

1/3 cup almonds

Olive oil

7oz firm tofu

3 tbsp nutritional yeast

Broth for blending

Fresh parsley

Lemon juice

Salt

Black pepper

Directions

Prep:
Preheat your oven to 180°C (356°F).

Place the halved red peppers, tomatoes on baking tray

Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Bake for about 25 minutes.

Cook
Blend the veggies and 8 garlic cloves.

Add tofu, a splash of olive oil, a handful of fresh parsley, 3 tablespoons of nutritional yeast, 1/3 cup of raw almonds, and the juice of one lemon.

Season again with a bit of salt and pepper to taste.

Add vegetable broth to help blend everything to a smooth consistency. You can adjust the amount to make the sauce as thick or thin as you like.

Use the sauce with cooked chickpeas, drizzled over yogurt, mixed with broccoli pesto, or as a creamy pasta sauce.

For a gourmet touch, sprinkle toasted almond flakes and vegan parmesan on top before serving.
