Snake Gourd Kootu

Indian, Raks Kitchen

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: 3 Source: rakskitchen.net

Ingredients

2 cups Snake gourd

¼ cup Chana dal

2 tablespoon Moong dal

Turmeric

Salt


Oil

Mustard seeds

Urad Dal

Curry leaves

Directions

Soak chana dal in water for 1 hour.

Clean and cut the snake gourd to your desired size cubes. I chopped little fine.

Take both the dals in a small pressure cooker and just add enough water to immerse the dal. Add turmeric and stir well.

Add the chopped veggie over it (don’t stir)

Pressure cook for 3 whistles in medium flame. Allow pressure to release naturally.

Add kootu powder and salt and bring to boil for a few minutes.

Add tempering items: mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves.
