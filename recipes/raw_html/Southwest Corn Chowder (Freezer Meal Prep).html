Southwest Corn Chowder (Freezer Meal Prep)

Ingredients

1 bell pepper, red

1 bag corn

1 cup dried red Lentils

5 garlic cloves

1/2 red onion

Paprika

Cumin powder

Garlic powder

1 can coconut milk

3 cup broth

Salt

Black pepper

Directions

Meal Prep Instructions
Label your gallon-sized freezer bag. Make sure to include name, instructions, and a use-by date (I recommend 90 days after prep date).
Add all of the ingredients (except for the coconut milk, and broth) to a freezer bag. Stir gently to combine.
Remove air, seal, and freeze.

Instant Pot Instructions
Thaw the freezer meal overnight in the fridge.
From thawed, add the coconut milk, and broth, stir to combine and then pressure cook on high for 10 minutes, then natural release for 10 minutes. Add salt as desired.

Use an immersion blender inside of the Instant Pot, or transfer 1/2 of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth. Return to Instant Pot.

Tip: Garnish with sliced jalapeno, freshly chopped tomatoes, and your favorite hot sauce.
