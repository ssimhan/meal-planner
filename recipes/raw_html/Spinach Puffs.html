Spinach Puffs

★★★★★

Guest Apps, School Entrees

Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 1 minutes Servings: 4 to 6 servings Source: Bonappetit.com

Ingredients

3-4 cups spinach

1/2 cup crumbled feta

1/4 cup minced onion, optional

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 teaspoon chopped dill

1 teaspoon minced garlic

Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

2 eggs

1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed

Directions

Cut and saute spinach until wilted. Squeeze out any remaining water.

Take puff pastry out to thaw on counter.

Mix spinach, feta, onion, oil, dill and garlic in a medium bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. In a small bowl, beat 1 egg to blend; fold into spinach mixture.

Preheat oven to 400°.

Roll out pastry on lightly floured surface.

Cut out large squares and place one in each muffin cup, pressing into bottom and up sides and leaving corners pointing up.

Divide filling among cups. Fold pastry over filling, pressing corners together to meet in center.

Beat second egg to blend in a small bowl. Brush pastry with egg wash (this will give the pastry a nice sheen). Alternatively use oil or butter here instead.

Bake until pastry is golden brown and puffed, about 25 minutes.

Let them cool on counter for 10 minutes before serving.
