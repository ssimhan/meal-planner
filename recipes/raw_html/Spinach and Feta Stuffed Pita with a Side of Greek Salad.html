Spinach and Feta Stuffed Pita with a Side of Greek Salad

Middle Eastern, Salad, Sandwich

Ingredients

2 cups spinach

Feta cheese

1/2 red onion

Garlic cloves

3 whole wheat pitas

1 cucumber

2 tomatoes

Kalamata olives

Lemon juice

Dried oregano

Directions

Prep:
Roughly chop 2 cups of fresh spinach.

Combine the chopped spinach with 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese, 1/4 cup of finely chopped onions, 1 minced garlic clove, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well.

Cut 2-3 whole wheat pitas in half, creating pockets.

Cook
Carefully spoon the spinach and feta mixture into each pita pocket, being careful not to overfill.

In a large skillet or grill pan over medium heat, add a little olive oil. Cook the stuffed pitas for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the outsides are golden and the filling is warmed through.

Greek Salad Prep:
Dice 1 cucumber, 2 tomatoes, and 1/4 of a red onion. Place them in a bowl.

Add 1/4 cup of kalamata olives and 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese to the vegetables.

In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and a pinch of salt and pepper.

Combine and serve
