Directions

Choose a protein (1 pound)
Tofu: cubed, extra firm
Seiten: chopped
Tempeh: chopped

Choose aromatics (1 tablespoon)
Garlic, shallots, green onions, ginger

Prep vegetables (4 cups)
Onions - thinly sliced
Bell peppers - sliced
Carrots - thinly sliced
Broccoli - chopped and microwaved with damp paper towel for 2 minutes
Baby spinach
Frozen peas, edamame or corn
Sugar snap peas, trimmed and left whole
Bean sprouts
Mushrooms - sliced
Green beans - trimmed
Baby corn - drained and rinsed

Make the sauce
Options listed in infographic: https://www.cooksmarts.com/articles/guide-to-stir-frying/

Make the stir fry
Heat a wok over medium high heat.

Once hot, add 2 tbsp sesame oil and protein and let sit for 4 minutes before tossing to coat in oil.

Saute until tofu is golden, remove from pan and set aside.

Wipe wok with paper towel if needed, return to medium-high heat.

Add cooking oil and aromatics (garlic, shallots, green onion, ginger).

Once you can smell them, add veggies to heated oil with sprinkle of salt.

Stir fry for 10 minutes until tender but still crunchy. If pan ever looks too dry, add splash of water or broth

Add protein back to wok, push all ingredients to sides forming a hole int he middle.

Place sauce in the hole, wait for it to bubble and darken before tossing everything through and making sure ll the ingredients are coated.

Cook until all ingredients are tender, then remove from heat.

Garnish with lemon juice, salt, cilantro, basil, green onion, cashews or peanuts.
