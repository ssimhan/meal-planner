Strawberry Muffins

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: 8 Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

½ cup milk

¼ cup canola oil

1 large egg

1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour

½ cup white sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon salt

1 cup chopped strawberries

Directions

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)

Lightly beat milk, oil, and egg in a small bowl.

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.

Toss in chopped strawberries and stir to coat with flour.

Fold in the milk mixture until just combined. Can add in some vanilla extract too if desired.

Divide batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups.

Bake in the preheated oven until the tops bounce back from the touch and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool for 10 minutes in the pan before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
