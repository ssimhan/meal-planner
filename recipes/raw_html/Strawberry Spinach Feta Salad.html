Strawberry Spinach Feta Salad

★★★★

Salad

Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Yield: 4 - 5 servings Source: Herwholesomekitchen.com

Ingredients

3 cups halved strawberries (about 1 pound)

½ cup crumbled feta (about 4 ounces)

½ red onion, thinly sliced

¾ cup pecans, chopped

1 cup diced cucumber

5 ounces spinach


2 tablespoons honey

1 tablespoon dijon

1 garlic clove, minced

1 shallot, finely diced

¼ cup balsamic vinegar

¾ cup olive oil

½ teaspoon salt

⅛ teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Add all of the ingredients for the salad to a large bowl.

In a small bowl or jar, mix together the dressing ingredients.

Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss. Start by using half of the dressing and then add more depending on personal preference.
