Tandoori Veggie Quesadillas

★★★★★

Dinner, TCL

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Source: Thechutneylife.com

Ingredients

1 bell pepper, red

1 bell pepper, green

1 onion, red

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 tbsp garlic, minced


1 tsp garam masala

1 tsp taco seasoning

1 tsp paprika

1 tsp red chili

1 pinch cinnamon, optional

1/4 cup cilantro

1 cup frozen corn, rinsed and thawed

Salt to taste

2 tbsp oil

1 package shredded mexican blend cheese

1 package tortillas


1 cup cream cheese or Greek yogurt

2 tsp sriracha sauce

Directions

Set corn to cook in microwave.

Dice peppers and onion.

Set up rotimatic if using. Adults need 3-4, kids need 2-3.

Heat a large wide skillet over medium high heat. Add oil. Once oil is hot add the cumin seeds and let splutter for about 10-15 seconds.

Add the sliced peppers and onions and sauté until slightly softened (about 4-5 minutes)

Drain the cooked corn and let it cool.

Add garlic, dry seasonings, and salt to the pan - reduce heat to low so the spices do not burn.

Add corn, cilantro and stir well. Taste test for spice and salt levels. Remove from heat.

Mix cream cheese and Sriracha sauce.

In a large pan heat up a little bit of oil and add one tortilla spread with a layer of the cream cheese mixture.

Top with cheese, a few spoonfuls of the veggie filling, additional cheese and fold the tortilla over.

Press in panini/sandwich maker for 1 minute. Remove from heat, cut and serve hot!
