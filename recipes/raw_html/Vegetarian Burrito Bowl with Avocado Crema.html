Vegetarian Burrito Bowl with Avocado Crema

★★★★★

Mexican

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 30 min Servings: Serves: 6 Source: themodernproper.com

Ingredients

1 head of cauliflower

1 green bell pepper

1 red bell pepper

½ onion

⅓ cup olive oil

2 tbsp lime juice

¼ cup Taco seasoning

2 cans black beans, refried

½ cup salsa

2 cups romaine lettuce

Extra cilantro for serving

4 cups cilantro-lime rice


Avocado Crema

1 Avocado

1 Garlic clove, minced

1/2 cup Sour cream

1/2 tsp salt

3 tbsp Lime juice

1/4 cup Cilantro


1 cup extra long grain rice or basmati rice

1 1/4 cups water

2 tsp olive oil

1 tsp salt

1/4 cup cilantro

3 tsp vegetable oil

3 tbsp lime juice

Directions

Make cilantro lime rice
Instant Pot: 1 cup rice, 1 1/4 water, 1 tsp olive oil, salt

High pressure for 3 minutes, natural pressure release for 10 minutes. Total time should be roughly 20 minutes

Combine chopped cilantro, lime juice, and some more oil. Fluff rice with fork and toss the mix together.

Burrito Bowl
Preheat the oven to 400°F.

Chop cauliflower, bell peppers, onion. Drain black beans.

In a large bowl toss cauliflower, onion, and bell peppers with the taco seasoning, olive oil and lime juice then spread out on a large (rimmed) baking sheet.

Place on the center rack and bake for 30 minutes or until the cauliflower is tender with crispy edges.

While the vegetables are cooking prepare avocado crema by blending avocado, garlic, sour cream, salt, lime juice, cilantro until smooth. Also chop the lettuce.

In a small skillet combine black beans and salsa over medium heat and cook for 5 minutes, until warmed through.

Serve the roasted taco vegetables over cilantro rice, with the beans, lettuce and avocado crema.
