Zucchini and Paneer Waffles

Source: instagram.com

Ingredients

Shredded Zucchini

Salt

Red chili powder

Turmeric

Amchur powder

Shredded paneer

Cilantro

Olive oil

Rice flour

Directions

Shred the Zucchini and add salt to it and leave it for 10 minutes

Drain and squeeze the water out of the Zucchini

Add chopped cilantro and shredded paneer

Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, amchur powder and salt only if needed

Add the rice flour and mix it well to form a thick batter (the longer the batter sits you may need more rice flour)

Grease the waffle maker with oil

Spread the batter on the waffle maker and cook for 10 minutes

Flip it mid way and cook on the other side

Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve it along with Dill and Pepper Dip
